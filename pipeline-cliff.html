⚠ Methodology & caveats方法與注意事項
Quarterly delivery profiles built from official supply reports (CMHC, Colliers, C&W, NAPIC, Cotality, REIC, BSP) plus developer announcements. Slippage is the rule — Dubai's 2026 record showed only 48% of forecast units actually completing on schedule. We modeled with that slippage where the source provided it; otherwise straight-line allocation. Use as directional signal, not a precise spreadsheet. 每季交屋曲線由各國官方供給報告（CMHC、Colliers、C&W、NAPIC、Cotality、REIC、BSP）＋開發商公告建立。延遲是常態 — 杜拜 2026 紀錄顯示僅 48% 預定交屋準時完成。來源提供時採用實際延遲率，否則線性分布。視為方向訊號，非精確試算表。
