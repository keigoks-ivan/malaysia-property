SUMMARY總結

Affordability Crisis可負擔性危機

At 85 years of median income to buy a median house, Sydney remains one of the world's least affordable cities. Policy intervention risk remains elevated.以中位收入購買中位房價需85年，雪梨仍為全球最難負擔城市之一。政策干預風險持續偏高。

Rental Strength租賃強勢

Weekly rents at A$817 are the highest in Australia. Five-year rent growth of +42.9% reflects chronic undersupply and record migration.每週租金A$817為全澳最高。五年租金成長+42.9%反映長期供給不足與創紀錄移民。

East-West Divide東西差距

Eastern Suburbs command 3x the price of Western Sydney. Parramatta's emergence as a second CBD is narrowing this gap over time.東區房價為西雪梨的3倍。帕拉馬塔崛起為第二CBD正逐步縮小此差距。

Yield Strategy收益策略

Units at 4.0% yield significantly outperform houses at 2.9%. Western Sydney offers the best income returns for investors seeking cash flow.公寓4.0%收益率顯著優於獨棟2.9%。西雪梨為追求現金流投資者提供最佳收入回報。
