EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Australia's residential property market entered 2026 in a state of extreme structural tension. Total dwelling value hit a record A$12.307 trillion in December 2025, with the national average price reaching A$1,074,700. The RBA raised the cash rate to 4.10% on 17 March 2026 — the second consecutive hike — driven by sticky inflation and Middle East energy shocks, with ASX futures pricing rates as high as 4.75% by early 2027. Yet despite this aggressive monetary tightening, structural undersupply is proving immune to rate signals. National completions fell 12.5% YoY in Q2 2025. The National Housing Accord's 1.2M target is tracking at just 78% achievable. Vacancy rates sit at just 1.1% nationally — Perth 0.6%, Brisbane 1.0%, Sydney 1.5%. The market is running a pronounced two-speed dynamic: Brisbane (+17.3% YoY), Perth (+22.0%), and Gold Coast (+12.8%) continue delivering double-digit growth; while Sydney (+6.0%) and Melbourne (+4.7%) have moderated as affordability hits hard ceilings. Resale profitability reached a 20-year high — 95.5% of sellers made a nominal gain in Q3 2025, with a median profit of A$335,000. The crisis is structural, not cyclical.

澳洲住宅房市在2026年的高度結構性張力中啟動。2025年12月全國住宅總值創下A$12.307兆的歷史紀錄，全國平均價格達到A$1,074,700。RBA在2026年3月17日將現金利率升至4.10%——連續第二次升息，主因為通膨黏性與中東能源衝擊，ASX期貨定價顯示2027年初可能升至4.75%。然而，儘管貨幣緊縮積極，結構性供給短缺已對利率信號形成免疫。2025年Q2全國完工量年減12.5%。《國家住房協議》120萬套目標預計僅能達成78%。全國空置率僅1.1%——伯斯0.6%、布里斯本1.0%、雪梨1.5%。市場呈現顯著的雙速格局：布里斯本（年增+17.3%）、伯斯（+22.0%）和黃金海岸（+12.8%）持續交出雙位數增長；雪梨（+6.0%）和墨爾本（+4.7%）因可負擔性觸頂而放緩。轉售獲利率達20年新高——2025年Q3有95.5%的賣家錄得名目利潤，中位獲利A$335,000。這是一場結構性危機，而非週期性波動。

RBA & MACRO: MARCH 2026 RATE HIKERBA與總體環境：2026年3月升息

The Reserve Bank of Australia raised the cash rate by 25 bps to 4.10% on 17 March 2026, following the February hike to 3.85%. This was triggered by a February trimmed-mean CPI of 4.1%, well above the 2-3% target band. ASX 30-day interbank futures now price a terminal rate of 4.75% by Q1 2027. The average new variable mortgage rate has risen to 6.75%, with some lenders above 7%. Mortgage stress — defined as households spending over 30% of income on repayments — now affects 47.5% of mortgage holders nationally, reaching 56.8% in NSW. Despite this, distressed listings remain suppressed at 29.2% below pre-COVID levels, as equity buffers and employment resilience (unemployment at 4.3%) prevent forced selling.

澳洲儲備銀行在2026年3月17日將現金利率上調25個基點至4.10%，緊隨2月份升至3.85%。此次升息由2月份截尾均值CPI達4.1%所觸發，遠高於2-3%的目標區間。ASX 30天銀行間期貨目前定價終端利率在2027年第一季達到4.75%。新增浮動房貸平均利率已升至6.75%，部分銀行超過7%。房貸壓力——定義為家庭將超過30%收入用於還款——目前影響全國47.5%的房貸持有者，在新南威爾斯州達到56.8%。儘管如此，困境房源仍被壓制在低於疫情前29.2%的水平，因為權益緩衝和就業韌性（失業率4.3%）防止了強制拋售。

SUPPLY: STRUCTURAL DEFICIT供給：結構性赤字

Australia's housing construction pipeline is in crisis. Total dwelling completions in the year to June 2025 fell to 174,271 — well below the National Housing Accord target of 240,000 per annum. The annualised completion rate in Q2 2025 dropped to just 162,096, a 12.5% YoY decline. Building approvals plunged in January 2026, with total approvals at 14,564 (down from a November peak of 18,406) and apartment approvals collapsing 24.5% month-on-month to 4,393. Construction timelines have blown out dramatically: WA now averages 15.45 months to complete a house, up 71% from 9.06 months in 2020. The NHSAC projects a cumulative supply-demand gap of -165,000 dwellings over the Accord period (2024-2029), making the 1.2M target virtually unachievable.

澳洲住宅建設在建量正陷入危機。截至2025年6月的年度住宅完工量降至174,271套——遠低於《國家住房協議》每年240,000套的目標。2025年Q2年化完工率僅162,096套，年減12.5%。2026年1月建築批准大幅下滑，總批准數為14,564套（低於11月峰值18,406套），公寓批准月減24.5%至4,393套。施工時間大幅延長：西澳目前平均完工一棟住宅需15.45個月，較2020年的9.06個月增長71%。NHSAC預計協議期間（2024-2029）累計供需缺口達-165,000套，使120萬套目標幾乎無法實現。

DEMAND: MIGRATION, CREDIT & WEALTH EFFECTS需求：移民、信貸與財富效應

Net overseas migration (NOM) peaked at 556,000 in FY2023, the highest on record, before moderating to 446,000 in FY2024 and an estimated 306,000 in FY2025. Even at moderated levels, NOM continues to far exceed annual housing supply. FIRB-approved foreign purchases fell to 3,922 in FY2025, with Chinese investment dropping to A$1.4 billion. Credit flows are increasingly dominated by existing property — 56.7% of new lending is for owner-occupied existing dwellings, while new construction attracts only 15.7%. Debt-to-income ratios have hit alarming levels: Gold Coast leads at 11.2x, followed by Sydney at 10.8x, both well above APRA's benchmark of 6x.

淨海外移民（NOM）在2023財政年度達到創紀錄的556,000人，隨後降至2024年的446,000人和2025年估計的306,000人。即使在放緩水平，NOM仍遠超年度住房供應。FIRB批准的外國購買降至2025年的3,922宗，中國投資降至A$14億。信貸流向日益集中於存量物業——56.7%的新增貸款用於自住型現有住宅，而新建僅佔15.7%。債務收入比已達驚人水平：黃金海岸以11.2倍領先，雪梨10.8倍緊隨其後，均遠超APRA的6倍基準。

VALUATION & AFFORDABILITY估值與可負擔性

Gross rental yields remain deeply compressed relative to financing costs, with the national average at 3.7% versus a mortgage rate of 6.75%. Sydney houses yield just 3.1%, while Darwin leads at 6.6%. The market is bifurcating by price quartile: lower-quartile properties delivered +2.5% growth, while upper-quartile properties fell -2.0% — a clear affordability gravity effect. Build-to-rent (BTR) is emerging as the institutional answer to the rental crisis, with the pipeline growing from 800 units in 2021 to 5,500 in 2025 and projected to reach 18,000 by 2030. Rent inflation continues to outpace CPI, with actual rents rising 6.6% in 2025 versus CPI of 4.2%.

毛租金收益率相對融資成本仍深度壓縮，全國平均為3.7%，而房貸利率為6.75%。雪梨獨立屋僅3.1%，而達爾文以6.6%領先。市場正按價格四分位數分化：低四分位物業實現+2.5%增長，而高四分位物業下跌-2.0%——明顯的可負擔性引力效應。建後出租（BTR）正成為機構應對租賃危機的方案，在建量從2021年的800套增長至2025年的5,500套，預計2030年達到18,000套。租金通膨持續超越CPI，2025年實際租金上漲6.6%，而CPI為4.2%。

SYDNEY: AFFORDABILITY CEILING雪梨：可負擔性天花板

Sydney remains the nation's most expensive market, with the median house price reaching A$1,607,046 in Q1 2026 and units hitting A$903,080. Annual growth has moderated to +6.0% as affordability constraints intensify. Auction clearance rates have been volatile — surging to 79.6% in January 2026 before collapsing to 55% in March post-rate hike. The market is increasingly two-speed within Sydney itself: the lower quartile continues to find buyers, while prestige properties face longer days on market and deeper vendor discounting.

雪梨仍是全國最昂貴的市場，2026年Q1中位獨立屋價格達A$1,607,046，公寓達A$903,080。年增率放緩至+6.0%，因可負擔性限制加劇。拍賣清算率波動劇烈——2026年1月飆升至79.6%，然後在升息後3月暴跌至55%。雪梨內部市場也日益雙速化：低價位段持續有買家，而高端物業面臨更長的上市天數和更深的賣方折讓。

BRISBANE: OLYMPIC CITY MOMENTUM布里斯本：奧運城市動能

Brisbane is the standout performer of the cycle, with house prices reaching A$1,080,000 (up 17.3% YoY) and units at A$680,000. The 2032 Olympics continues to drive infrastructure and investor sentiment. Key Olympic precincts are commanding significant premiums: Bowen Hills leads at +28% above pre-announcement levels, followed by Herston/Kelvin Grove at +25%. The city's vacancy rate of 1.0% is among the tightest nationally, with median rents up 8.0% YoY. Interstate migration from Sydney and Melbourne continues to underpin demand.

布里斯本是本輪週期中表現最突出的城市，獨立屋價格達A$1,080,000（年增17.3%），公寓達A$680,000。2032年奧運會持續推動基礎設施和投資者信心。主要奧運區域溢價顯著：Bowen Hills以高於公布前水平+28%領先，其次是Herston/Kelvin Grove的+25%。該市空置率1.0%為全國最緊張之一，中位租金年增8.0%。來自雪梨和墨爾本的州際移民持續支撐需求。

GOLD COAST: LIFESTYLE PREMIUM LOGIC黃金海岸：生活方式溢價邏輯

The Gold Coast has undergone a structural repricing, with median unit prices reaching A$956,000 in October 2025 — overtaking Sydney units at A$927,000 for the first time in history. This convergence reflects the lifestyle premium that post-COVID migration patterns have crystallised. Annual growth of +12.8% is supported by a vacancy rate of just 0.8% and a debt-to-income ratio of 11.2x — the highest in Australia. However, the apartment supply pipeline is heading for a cliff: completions drop from 1,870 in 2025 to just 95 in 2027, which will intensify the supply crisis further.

黃金海岸經歷了結構性重定價，2025年10月中位公寓價格達A$956,000——歷史上首次超越雪梨公寓的A$927,000。此收斂反映了疫情後遷移模式所固化的生活方式溢價。年增+12.8%由僅0.8%的空置率和11.2倍的債務收入比（全澳最高）所支撐。然而公寓在建供給正走向斷崖：完工量從2025年的1,870套降至2027年僅95套，將進一步加劇供應危機。

OUTLOOK: THREE SCENARIOS FOR 2026-2027展望：2026-2027年三大情境

We model three scenarios for the next 12-18 months. Bull case (25% probability): RBA cuts to 3.50% by mid-2027, migration stays above 300K, completions remain below 170K — national prices +12%. Base case (50% probability): RBA holds at 4.10-4.35%, migration normalises to 260K, construction slowly recovers — national prices +6%. Bear case (25% probability): RBA hikes to 4.75%, global recession triggers migration pullback, distressed listings rise — national prices -3%. In all scenarios, Perth, Brisbane, and Gold Coast outperform due to structural undersupply and demographic tailwinds. Melbourne and Sydney underperform on affordability constraints. Rents continue rising 5-12% in all scenarios.

我們為未來12-18個月建立三種情境模型。牛市情境（25%概率）：RBA在2027年中降至3.50%，移民維持30萬以上，完工量低於17萬——全國房價+12%。基本情境（50%概率）：RBA維持在4.10-4.35%，移民正常化至26萬，建設緩慢恢復——全國房價+6%。熊市情境（25%概率）：RBA升至4.75%，全球衰退觸發移民回撤，困境房源上升——全國房價-3%。在所有情境中，伯斯、布里斯本和黃金海岸因結構性供不應求和人口順風而跑贏。墨爾本和雪梨因可負擔性限制而表現不佳。租金在所有情境中持續上漲5-12%。
