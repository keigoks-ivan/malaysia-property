澳洲住宅房地產市場報告

2026年3月 · 市場研究

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Australia's residential property market entered 2026 in a renewed upswing, with the national median dwelling value reaching A$922,838 by February 2026, up +9.9% year-on-year. The market is running a two-speed dynamic: Brisbane and the Gold Coast continue to deliver double-digit annual growth driven by lifestyle migration, the 2032 Olympics pipeline, and near-zero vacancy rates, while Sydney and Melbourne have moderated to low-single-digit appreciation as affordability constraints increasingly bite. The RBA raised the cash rate to 3.85% in February 2026, but structural undersupply — with rental listings 17% below five-year averages and vacancy at just 1.7% nationally — continues to underpin both price and rental growth. National rental yields of 4.69% produce a spread of +0.84% over the cash rate — thin but positive. Over the past five years, national rents have surged +42.9%, and Sydney now takes an estimated 85 years to own on median income.

澳洲住宅房市在2026年進入新一輪升勢，全國住宅中位價值於2026年2月達到A$922,838，年增+9.9%。市場呈現雙速格局：布里斯本和黃金海岸受惠於生活方式遷移、2032奧運基建管線及接近零的空置率，持續交出雙位數年增長；而雪梨和墨爾本則因可負擔性壓力加劇，漲幅放緩至低個位數。澳洲儲備銀行（RBA）2026年2月升息至3.85%，但結構性供不應求——租賃掛牌量較五年均值低17%，全國空置率僅1.7%——繼續支撐房價和租金成長。全國租金收益率4.69%與現金利率之間的利差為+0.84%——微薄但仍為正值。過去五年全國租金飆升+42.9%，雪梨以中位收入購房估計需85年。

RBA: FEBRUARY 2026 RATE HIKERBA：2026年2月升息

The Reserve Bank of Australia raised the cash rate to 3.85% in February 2026 after a six-month pause, citing persistent housing-driven CPI as the primary justification. Housing costs remain the single largest contributor to headline inflation, with rents and new dwelling purchase costs running well above the RBA's 2–3% target band. Consumer confidence fell sharply to 82.9 on the Westpac index following the announcement, reflecting household anxiety over mortgage servicing costs. The rate hike widens the gap between borrowing costs and wage growth, further constraining first-home-buyer purchasing power while doing little to address the structural supply deficit that is driving housing inflation in the first place.

澳洲儲備銀行在暫停六個月後，於2026年2月升息至3.85%，理由是住房驅動的CPI持續頑固。住房成本仍是整體通膨的最大單一貢獻項，租金和新住宅購買成本遠高於RBA 2–3%的目標區間。加息公告後，Westpac消費者信心指數驟降至82.9，反映家庭對房貸還款成本的焦慮。升息拉大了借貸成本與薪資增長之間的差距，進一步壓縮首次購房者的購買力，卻無法解決推動住房通膨的結構性供給赤字。

SUPPLY: CHRONIC DEFICIT供給：慢性赤字

Australia's housing construction pipeline is in structural deficit. Annual dwelling starts have fallen to approximately 158,000 against the federal government's target of 240,000 per year — a gap of 82,000 homes annually. Construction costs have risen 25–29% since 2020, squeezing builder margins and contributing to a wave of insolvencies. Rental listings remain 17% below five-year averages, and the national vacancy rate sits at just 1.7%, well below the 3.0% threshold associated with a balanced rental market. CBRE forecasts that Sydney apartment completions will not meet demand before 2029, ensuring the rental squeeze persists. Until completions rise materially above 200,000 annually, price and rental growth will remain supported by simple scarcity.

澳洲住宅建設管線處於結構性赤字。年住宅開工量已降至約158,000套，與聯邦政府每年240,000套的目標相比，缺口達82,000套。自2020年以來建築成本上漲25–29%，擠壓建商利潤並引發一波破產潮。租賃掛牌量較五年均值低17%，全國空置率僅1.7%，遠低於代表租賃市場平衡的3.0%門檻。CBRE預測雪梨公寓竣工量在2029年之前無法滿足需求，確保租賃擠壓持續。在年竣工量大幅超過20萬套之前，房價和租金成長將持續受到稀缺性支撐。

DEMAND: MIGRATION WAVE AND INVESTOR DOMINANCE需求：移民潮與投資者主導

Property transfers reached 565,073 nationally in the year to February 2026, up +2.6% year-on-year. Net overseas migration exceeded 500,000 in 2023–24, and while the pace has moderated, each year's inflow still far exceeds the housing construction run rate. Investors now account for 42% of new mortgage commitments, the highest share since the 2015 boom, drawn by positive yield spreads and expectations of capital appreciation. The demand composition is shifting southeast: Queensland's net interstate migration of 104,842 reflects the lifestyle arbitrage enabled by remote work, with families and investors alike repricing Brisbane and the Gold Coast relative to Sydney and Melbourne. The demand picture is broad-based — owner-occupiers, investors, and migrants are all competing for a structurally limited housing stock.

截至2026年2月的一年內，全國房產過戶量達565,073宗，年增+2.6%。2023–24年度海外淨移民超過50萬，儘管增速已放緩，但每年流入量仍遠超住宅建設速度。投資者目前佔新房貸承諾的42%，為2015年繁榮以來最高佔比，受正利差和資本增值預期吸引。需求構成正向東南方轉移：昆士蘭淨跨州遷入104,842人，反映遠程工作所實現的生活方式套利，家庭和投資者都在重新定價布里斯本和黃金海岸相對於雪梨和墨爾本的價值。需求面呈現全方位態勢——自住者、投資者和移民都在爭奪結構性有限的住房存量。

VALUATION: AMONG WORLD'S LEAST AFFORDABLE估值：全球最難負擔之列

Australia's national rental yield of 4.69% against the RBA cash rate of 3.85% produces a spread of +0.84% — positive but thin by historical standards. Sydney houses yield just 2.9%, implying negative carry for leveraged investors after financing costs. The national dwelling-value-to-income ratio has reached 8.2x, placing Australia among the world's least affordable housing markets. Brisbane offers a more attractive yield profile at 3.6% for houses, while the Gold Coast delivers 4.3–5.0% across houses and units — the strongest carry in any capital-adjacent market. For income-focused investors, the southeast Queensland corridor remains the standout opportunity, combining positive carry with above-trend capital growth.

澳洲全國租金收益率4.69%扣除RBA現金利率3.85%後，利差為+0.84%——為正但以歷史標準偏薄。雪梨獨立屋收益率僅2.9%，扣除融資成本後對槓桿投資者意味著負利差。全國住宅價值對收入比已達8.2倍，使澳洲躋身全球最難負擔的住宅市場之列。布里斯本獨立屋收益率3.6%較具吸引力，而黃金海岸跨獨立屋和公寓提供4.3–5.0%的收益率——為所有首府城市鄰近市場中最強利差。對收租導向投資者而言，東南昆士蘭走廊仍是最突出的機會，兼具正利差與高於趨勢的資本增長。

BRISBANE: OLYMPIC CITY MOMENTUM布里斯本：奧運城市動能

Brisbane is Australia's standout market. The median dwelling value has surged from A$700,000 to A$1.31 million over the past five years — a gain of +86.7%. The 2032 Olympic and Paralympic Games are the catalyst for a generational infrastructure program: Cross River Rail, the Brisbane Metro, and over A$7 billion in sporting and transport upgrades are transforming the city's urban fabric. Queensland recorded net interstate migration of 104,842 as remote work enables lifestyle arbitrage from Sydney and Melbourne. The vacancy rate has fallen to just 1.0%, driving rents up +5.2% year-on-year — and this follows several years of double-digit rent growth that has already repriced the market.

布里斯本是澳洲表現最突出的市場。過去五年住宅中位價值從A$700,000飆升至A$131萬——漲幅達+86.7%。2032年奧運會和殘奧會是一代人級別基建計畫的催化劑：跨河鐵路、布里斯本地鐵，以及超過70億澳元的體育和交通升級正在重塑城市面貌。昆士蘭錄得淨跨州遷入104,842人，遠程工作使從雪梨和墨爾本的生活方式套利成為可能。空置率已降至僅1.0%，推動租金年增+5.2%——而這是在此前數年雙位數租金增長已重新定價市場之後的漲幅。

GOLD COAST: LIFESTYLE PREMIUM LOGIC黃金海岸：生活方式溢價邏輯

The Gold Coast median dwelling value has reached A$1.17 million, up +10–12% year-on-year. The vacancy rate of 1.1% is the second tightest in the nation after Brisbane, and rental yields of 4.3–5.0% across houses and units provide the strongest carry in any capital-adjacent market. The population shift from Sydney and Melbourne continues to accelerate, driven by lifestyle preferences, remote work adoption, and the relative affordability compared to Sydney. The Airbnb and short-stay rental sector adds a further demand layer, particularly along the beachfront and in the northern corridor toward Brisbane. Infrastructure investment — including the Gold Coast light rail extension and 2032 Olympic venue upgrades — is reinforcing the structural growth story.

黃金海岸住宅中位價值已達A$117萬，年增+10–12%。空置率1.1%為全國第二低，僅次於布里斯本，租金收益率跨獨立屋和公寓達4.3–5.0%，為所有首府城市鄰近市場中最強利差。從雪梨和墨爾本的人口遷移持續加速，受生活方式偏好、遠程工作普及以及相對於雪梨的可負擔性驅動。Airbnb和短租領域為需求增添了另一層推動力，尤其是沿海濱地帶及向北通往布里斯本的走廊。基礎設施投資——包括黃金海岸輕軌延伸和2032奧運場館升級——正在強化結構性增長故事。

OUTLOOK展望

Bull case: RBA cuts rates in H2 2026, migration stays elevated, supply deficit widens. National prices +10–12%, Brisbane/Gold Coast +15–18%. Yield spreads widen further, drawing more investor capital.

Base case: RBA holds at 3.85% through 2026, moderate price growth of +5–8% nationally. Brisbane/Gold Coast outperform at +10–14%. Sydney moderates to +2–4%. Rents continue to rise +4–6% on supply constraints.

Bear case: Inflation re-accelerates, RBA hikes to 4.10–4.35%. Yield spreads turn negative, investor sentiment reverses. National prices flat to -3%. Brisbane/Gold Coast vulnerable to -5–8% correction from overextended valuations.

2032 tail risk: Olympic host cities have historically experienced post-event corrections. Sydney (2000) saw a -5–10% adjustment in the two years following the Games. Brisbane's current price trajectory implies significant pre-event appreciation, creating a potential correction window in 2033–2034.

樂觀情境：RBA於2026年下半年降息，移民維持高位，供給赤字擴大。全國房價+10–12%，布里斯本/黃金海岸+15–18%。利差進一步擴大，吸引更多投資者資金。

基本情境：RBA全年維持3.85%不變，全國溫和漲價+5–8%。布里斯本/黃金海岸以+10–14%領跑。雪梨放緩至+2–4%。租金在供給約束下持續上漲+4–6%。

悲觀情境：通膨重新加速，RBA升息至4.10–4.35%。利差轉負，投資者情緒逆轉。全國房價持平至-3%。布里斯本/黃金海岸因估值過度延伸而面臨-5–8%修正風險。

2032尾部風險：奧運主辦城市歷史上曾出現賽後修正。雪梨（2000年）在賽後兩年內經歷了-5–10%的調整。布里斯本當前的價格軌跡意味著顯著的賽前升值，在2033–2034年形成潛在修正窗口。
