RISK FACTORS風險因素

Regulatory Risk法規風險

HIGH高

The Renters Rights Act abolishes Section 21 no-fault evictions from May 2026, accelerating small landlord exits and tightening rental supply. EPC upgrade requirements add £10,000–£15,000 cost per non-compliant property, further squeezing landlord margins.租客權益法案自2026年5月起廢除第21條無過失驅逐，加速小型房東退出並收緊租賃供給。能源效能憑證（EPC）升級要求每套不合規房產增加1萬至1.5萬英鎊成本，進一步擠壓房東利潤。

Rate Sensitivity利率敏感度

HIGH高

Despite the BoE cutting cycle, 2-year fixed mortgage rates remain at ~4.5%, down from the 6.4% peak in 2023 but still well above the sub-2% levels of 2021. Leveraged investors face thin or negative carry in London, and mortgage affordability remains stretched for first-time buyers.儘管BoE進入降息週期，2年期固定房貸利率仍約為4.5%，雖已從2023年6.4%的峰值回落，但仍遠高於2021年不到2%的水準。槓桿投資者在倫敦面臨微薄或負利差，首次購房者的房貸負擔能力依然緊張。

Supply Deficit供給赤字

MEDIUM中

Annual completions of ~220,000 homes fall short of the government's 300,000 target. Planning delays, labour shortages, and rising construction costs continue to constrain supply. The deficit supports prices but worsens housing affordability and rental pressure.年竣工量約22萬套，低於政府30萬套目標。規劃延遲、勞動力短缺及建築成本上漲持續制約供給。赤字支撐房價，但惡化住房可負擔性和租賃壓力。

Affordability可負擔性

MEDIUM中

London's price-to-income ratio sits at 8.6x, with the national average at 6.5x. While northern cities offer better value, affordability remains a structural barrier for first-time buyers across much of England, particularly in the South East.倫敦房價所得比為8.6倍，全國平均為6.5倍。雖然北部城市提供更好的價值，但可負擔性仍是英格蘭大部分地區、尤其是東南部首次購房者的結構性障礙。
