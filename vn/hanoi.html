EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Hanoi's property market has been the standout performer in Vietnam in 2025, with average apartment prices surging to $2,900/m² (+12% YoY) — the fastest growth rate among major Vietnamese cities. Chronic supply shortage is the primary driver: new launches dropped 40% since 2022 due to prolonged permit approvals. Only ~8,000 units were launched in 2025. The northern industrial corridors housing Samsung, LG, and Foxconn facilities generate steady demand from Korean and Taiwanese workers. Unlike HCMC, Hanoi's market is primarily domestic-driven, with foreign buyers accounting for ~8% of transactions.

河內房產市場是2025年越南表現最突出的，公寓均價飆升至$2,900/平方米（年增12%）——越南主要城市中增速最快。慢性供應短缺是主要驅動力：由於許可審批延長，2022年以來新推案量下降40%。2025年僅推出約8,000套。北部工業走廊的三星、LG和富士康工廠為韓國和台灣工人產生穩定住房需求。與胡志明市不同，河內市場主要由國內需求驅動，外國買家僅佔交易的約8%。

PRICE SURGE價格飆升

Hanoi apartment prices have risen 45% over 3 years (2022–2025), accelerating in 2024–2025. The CBD (Hoan Kiem, Ba Dinh) commands $4,200/m² for new projects. Western districts (Nam Tu Liem, Cau Giay) average $2,900/m² and are the primary supply zone. The price surge is supply-driven rather than speculative — vacancy rates are below 5% and rental demand remains strong. Secondary market transactions now dominate as new supply is insufficient.

河內公寓價格在3年內上漲45%（2022-2025），2024-2025年加速。CBD（還劍、巴亭）新項目均價$4,200/平方米。西部區域（南慈廉、紙橋）平均$2,900/平方米，為主要供應區。價格飆升是供應驅動而非投機——空置率低於5%，租賃需求仍然強勁。隨新供應不足，二手市場交易現已主導。

KEY DISTRICTS重點地區

Hanoi's property market is shaped by four distinct district clusters, each with different investment profiles and growth trajectories. The CBD core commands premium pricing anchored by heritage and government presence, while emerging eastern districts offer higher yields and infrastructure-driven upside.

河內房產市場由四個不同的區域集群塑造，各有不同的投資特徵和增長軌跡。CBD核心區以歷史遺產和政府所在地為支撐享有溢價定價，而新興東部區域則提供更高收益率和基礎設施驅動的上行空間。

INFRASTRUCTURE基礎設施

Hanoi's infrastructure buildout is reshaping property dynamics. The Ring Road 4 project ($3.5B, 2024–2027) will open up eastern and southern districts. Metro Line 3 (Nhon–Hanoi Station) is testing with 2025 commercial operation target. The industrial zones north of Hanoi (Bac Ninh, Thai Nguyen) housing Samsung and Foxconn create spillover demand. Noi Bai Airport expansion and the Hanoi–Hai Phong expressway link strengthen the northern economic corridor.

河內基礎設施建設正在重塑房產格局。四環路項目（35億美元，2024-2027）將開發東部和南部區域。地鐵3號線（日新-河內站）正在測試，目標2025年商業運營。河內以北的工業區（北寧、太原）的三星和富士康工廠產生溢出需求。內排機場擴建和河內-海防高速公路連接強化北部經濟走廊。

SUPPLY CRISIS供應危機

Hanoi's supply crisis is structural. Only 8,000 units were launched in 2025, down from 20,000+ annually pre-2022. The bottleneck is legal — land use planning conflicts, environmental assessments, and fire safety approvals average 3–5 years. Developers have shifted focus to HCMC and resort markets. The 2024 Housing Law reforms are expected to gradually unlock delayed projects, but meaningful supply recovery is not expected before 2027–2028. This supply shortage is the primary price support.

河內的供應危機是結構性的。2025年僅推出8,000套，低於2022年前每年20,000+套。障礙在法律面——土地使用規劃衝突、環評和消防安全審批平均需3-5年。開發商已將重心轉向胡志明市和度假市場。2024年住房法改革預計逐步釋放延遲項目，但有意義的供應恢復預計在2027-2028年之前不會出現。供應短缺是價格支撐的主要因素。

RENTAL MARKET租賃市場

Hanoi's rental market yields ~5.0% gross for mid-range condos. A 2-bedroom in Cau Giay rents for $600–900/month; in CBD, $1,000–1,800/month. Demand is driven by domestic migration, Korean/Japanese corporate tenants near industrial zones, and the growing middle class. Occupancy rates exceed 95% in well-located buildings. Unlike HCMC, Hanoi rents have risen 8–10% annually, partially catching up to price growth.

河內租賃市場中檔公寓毛收益率約5.0%。紙橋兩房月租$600-900；CBD $1,000-1,800。需求受國內遷移、工業區附近的韓日企業租戶和不斷壯大的中產階級推動。區位良好的建築入住率超過95%。與胡志明市不同，河內租金年增8-10%，部分追趕房價增長。

OUTLOOK展望

Hanoi's price growth is expected to moderate to 8–10% in 2026 as base effects kick in, but remain above HCMC due to persistent supply constraints. The supply crisis will take 2–3 years to resolve. Ring Road 4 and Metro Line 3 will create new growth districts. Key risk: if all delayed projects launch simultaneously in 2027–2028, prices could see a temporary correction. Long-term fundamentals remain strong — Hanoi's population growth, industrial corridor expansion, and government as political capital ensure sustained demand.

隨基數效應顯現，河內2026年價格增長預計放緩至8-10%，但由於持續供應限制仍高於胡志明市。供應危機需2-3年解決。四環路和地鐵3號線將創造新增長區域。主要風險：若延遲項目在2027-2028年同時推出，價格可能出現暫時調整。長期基本面仍強——河內人口增長、工業走廊擴張和作為政治首都的地位確保持續需求。
