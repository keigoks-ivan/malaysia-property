EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Ho Chi Minh City is Vietnam's commercial capital and largest property market, accounting for ~60% of the country's real estate transaction value. In 2025, average apartment prices reached $3,800/m² (+8% YoY), with District 1 luxury commanding $5,800/m². The opening of Metro Line 1 in December 2024 has been transformative, boosting corridor property values by 15–20%. New supply recovered to ~15,000 units after severe legal bottlenecks in 2021–2023. Foreign buyers represent ~15% of new condo purchases, with Korean and Taiwanese investors dominating. The 30% foreign quota is near capacity in several CBD projects.

胡志明市是越南的商業首都和最大的房地產市場，佔全國房地產交易額的約60%。2025年，公寓均價達$3,800/平方米（年增8%），第一郡豪華公寓高達$5,800/平方米。2024年12月地鐵1號線開通具有變革性意義，使沿線物業價值提升15-20%。新供應在2021-2023年嚴重法律障礙後恢復至約15,000套。外國買家佔新公寓購買的約15%，韓國和台灣投資者主導。多個CBD項目的30%外國配額已接近上限。

PRICE TREND房價走勢

HCMC condo prices have doubled over the past decade, from ~$1,900/m² in 2015 to $3,800/m² in 2025. The luxury segment (D1, Binh Thanh waterfront) has outperformed, reaching $5,000–8,000/m². Mid-range condos in Thu Duc and District 7 offer $2,500–3,500/m² — the sweet spot for foreign investors seeking yield. Price growth moderated from 12% (2022) to 8% (2025) as supply recovered. Secondary market premiums of 10–15% exist in buildings near the 30% foreign cap.

胡志明市公寓價格在過去十年翻了一番，從2015年的約$1,900/平方米到2025年的$3,800/平方米。豪華板塊（第一郡、平盛濱水區）表現最佳，達$5,000-8,000/平方米。守德和第七郡的中檔公寓提供$2,500-3,500/平方米——外國投資者尋求收益的最佳區間。隨供應恢復，價格增長從12%（2022年）放緩至8%（2025年）。接近30%外國上限的建築存在10-15%的二手市場溢價。

KEY DISTRICTS重點地區

HCMC's property market is defined by four key districts, each offering distinct risk-return profiles for investors. District 1 remains the undisputed luxury core, while Thu Duc has emerged as the primary growth corridor thanks to Metro Line 1 and the tech sector presence.

胡志明市的房地產市場由四個核心區域定義，每個區域為投資者提供不同的風險回報特徵。第一郡仍是無可爭議的豪華核心，而守德則因地鐵1號線和科技產業進駐成為主要增長走廊。

METRO IMPACT地鐵影響

Metro Line 1 (Ben Thanh – Suoi Tien, 20km, 14 stations) opened December 2024, connecting D1 CBD to Thu Duc. Properties within 500m of stations have seen 15–20% appreciation since announcement. Line 2 (Ben Thanh – Tham Luong) is under construction with 2028 target. The metro system is expected to reshape the city's property premium map, making Thu Duc the primary growth corridor for the next decade.

地鐵1號線（濱城-仙泉，20公里，14站）2024年12月開通，連接第一郡CBD至守德。車站500米範圍內的物業自公佈以來升值15-20%。2號線（濱城-潭陵）正在施工，目標2028年完工。地鐵系統預計將重塑城市物業溢價版圖，使守德成為未來十年的主要增長走廊。

INVENTORY庫存

After severe supply constraints in 2021–2023 (only 12,000–18,000 units launched annually due to legal bottlenecks), HCMC has seen gradual recovery. 2025 saw ~15,000 new units, primarily in Thu Duc and District 7. Unsold inventory has declined to ~10,000 units from a peak of 25,000 in 2020. The legal framework reforms of 2024 are expected to unlock ~20,000 delayed units by 2027. Absorption rates are healthy at 85–90% for well-located projects.

在2021-2023年嚴重供應受限（因法律障礙年推案僅12,000-18,000套）後，胡志明市逐步恢復。2025年推出約15,000套新單位，主要在守德和第七郡。未售庫存從2020年峰值25,000套降至約10,000套。2024年法律框架改革預計到2027年釋放約20,000套延遲項目。區位良好的項目吸收率健康在85-90%。

RENTAL MARKET租賃市場

HCMC rental market is buoyant, driven by expat demand and young professionals. Gross yields average 5.5% for mid-range condos, 4.5% for luxury D1 units, and up to 7% for serviced apartments. A 2-bedroom in Thu Duc rents for $800–1,200/month; in D1, $1,500–2,500/month. Occupancy rates remain above 90% in expat-popular areas. The growing tech sector (Samsung, Intel) in Thu Duc is driving premium rental demand.

胡志明市租賃市場活躍，受外籍人士需求和年輕專業人士推動。中檔公寓毛收益率平均5.5%，第一郡豪華單位4.5%，服務式公寓高達7%。守德兩房月租$800-1,200；第一郡$1,500-2,500。外籍人士熱門區域入住率維持90%以上。守德不斷壯大的科技產業（三星、英特爾）推動高端租賃需求。

OUTLOOK展望

HCMC's property market outlook is positive for 2026–2028. Catalysts include: Metro Line 1 ridership driving corridor demand, Long Thanh airport (2026 opening) creating a new growth axis, continued FDI inflows, and legal reforms unlocking supply. Risks include high mortgage rates limiting domestic participation and potential oversupply if all delayed projects launch simultaneously. Price growth forecast: 5–6% annually. Foreign demand expected to grow 15–20%/year as Vietnam's investment profile strengthens.

胡志明市房產市場2026-2028年前景積極。催化劑包括：地鐵1號線客流推動沿線需求、龍城機場（2026年開放）創造新增長軸線、持續FDI流入和法律改革釋放供應。風險包括高按揭利率限制國內參與度，以及延遲項目同時推出導致潛在供過於求。房價增長預測：年增5-6%。隨越南投資形象增強，外國需求預計年增15-20%。
