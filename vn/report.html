EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Vietnam's residential property market in 2026 is characterized by strong recovery momentum driven by economic fundamentals. GDP growth of 7.1%, FDI inflows of $23.7B, and a young population of 100M+ create robust demand. The 2024 Housing Law amendments extended foreign ownership leases to 50+50 years and expanded eligible property types. However, mortgage rates remain high at 9%, limiting domestic affordability. HCMC apartment prices averaged $3,800/m² (+8% YoY) while Hanoi surged to $2,900/m² (+12%). Foreign buyers, particularly from South Korea, China, and Taiwan, are increasingly active. The 30% foreign ownership quota per building remains the key structural constraint.

越南2026年住宅房地產市場以強勁的復甦動能為特徵，受經濟基本面推動。GDP增長7.1%、外國直接投資流入237億美元、1億+年輕人口創造強勁需求。2024年住房法修正案將外國人所有權租賃期延長至50+50年，並擴大合格房產類型。然而，按揭利率維持在9%的高位，限制國內負擔能力。胡志明市公寓均價達$3,800/平方米（年增8%），河內飆升至$2,900/平方米（年增12%）。韓國、中國和台灣買家日益活躍。每棟建築30%的外國人持有上限仍是關鍵結構性限制。

MACRO CONTEXT & GROWTH DRIVERS宏觀背景與增長驅動力

Vietnam stands out among ASEAN economies with consistent 6–7% GDP growth. The manufacturing boom driven by supply chain diversification from China has brought waves of Korean, Japanese, and Taiwanese workers, creating organic housing demand. FDI reached $23.7B in 2025 (+12% YoY). Urbanization rate at 38% is still low vs regional peers, implying decades of rural-to-urban migration ahead. The government's infrastructure push — including HCMC metro Line 1 (opened 2024), Long Thanh International Airport (2026), and North-South expressway — is reshaping property premiums.

越南在東盟經濟體中脫穎而出，GDP持續增長6–7%。由中國供應鏈多元化驅動的製造業繁榮帶來韓國、日本和台灣工人潮，創造有機住房需求。2025年FDI達237億美元（年增12%）。城市化率38%仍低於區域同行，意味著數十年的農村向城市遷移仍在前方。政府的基礎設施推動——包括胡志明市地鐵1號線（2024年開通）、龍城國際機場（2026年）和南北高速公路——正在重塑房產溢價。

FOREIGN OWNERSHIP FRAMEWORK外國所有權框架

Foreigners may own apartments (not landed property) within a 30% quota per building, with 50-year leasehold renewable once (total 100 years). The 2024 Housing Law expanded eligible buyers and property types. Key restrictions: cannot sub-lease to other foreigners, must remit purchase funds from abroad. Transfer to Vietnamese nationals is unrestricted. The 30% cap creates scarcity premiums in popular projects — some CBD buildings are already at quota, forcing foreign buyers to secondary markets.

外國人可持有公寓（非土地產權），每棟建築30%配額，50年租賃期可續約一次（共100年）。2024年住房法擴大了合格買家和房產類型。主要限制：不能轉租給其他外國人，購買資金須從海外匯入。轉讓給越南國民不受限制。30%上限在熱門項目中創造稀缺溢價——部分CBD大樓已達配額上限，迫使外國買家轉向二手市場。

HO CHI MINH CITY OVERVIEW胡志明市概覽

HCMC remains the primary market for foreign investors. District 1 luxury condos average $5,800/m², while Thu Duc (formerly D2) offers $3,800/m² with metro connectivity. New supply has recovered to ~15,000 units/year after COVID lows. Rental yields average 5.5% gross for mid-range condos, with serviced apartments reaching 7%+. The metro Line 1 opening in 2024 has boosted prices along the corridor by 15–20%.

胡志明市仍是外國投資者的主要市場。第一郡豪華公寓均價$5,800/平方米，守德（原第二郡）提供$3,800/平方米配以地鐵連接。新供應在COVID低點後恢復至每年約15,000套。中檔公寓毛租金收益率平均5.5%，服務式公寓達7%+。2024年地鐵1號線開通使沿線房價上漲15–20%。

HANOI OVERVIEW河內概覽

Hanoi has seen the sharpest price growth in 2025, with average apartment prices reaching $2,900/m² (+12% YoY). Supply shortage is acute — new launches dropped 40% since 2022 due to legal bottlenecks. Western districts (Nam Tu Liem, Cau Giay) dominate new supply. Rental yields are lower at ~5.0% due to faster price appreciation outpacing rents. The government's northern industrial corridors (Samsung, LG facilities) drive peripheral demand.

河內在2025年價格漲幅最為劇烈，公寓均價達$2,900/平方米（年增12%）。供應短缺嚴重——由於法律障礙，2022年以來新推案量下降40%。西部區域（南慈廉、紙橋）主導新供應。租金收益率較低約5.0%，因價格漲幅快於租金。政府的北部工業走廊（三星、LG工廠）推動外圍需求。

OUTLOOK 2026–2028展望 2026–2028

Vietnam's property market outlook is cautiously optimistic. Positives: strong GDP growth, young demographics, infrastructure buildout, and liberalized foreign ownership rules. Risks: high mortgage rates (9%), potential oversupply in resort markets (Da Nang, Nha Trang), and regulatory uncertainty. Price growth is expected to moderate to 5–6% annually in HCMC and 8–10% in Hanoi as supply catches up. The Long Thanh airport opening in 2026 could create a new growth corridor. Foreign demand is projected to increase 15–20% annually as Vietnam's profile rises among APAC investors.

越南房產市場前景審慎樂觀。正面因素：強勁GDP增長、年輕人口結構、基礎設施建設和放寬的外國人所有權規則。風險：高按揭利率（9%）、度假市場潛在供過於求（峴港、芽莊）及監管不確定性。預計胡志明市價格年增放緩至5–6%，河內8–10%，隨供應追趕。2026年龍城機場開放可能創造新增長走廊。隨越南在亞太投資者中知名度提升，外國需求預計每年增長15–20%。
