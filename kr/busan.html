EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Busan is South Korea's second-largest city and principal port, home to 3.4 million residents. The average apartment price sits at approximately ₩350M ($250K), up +3% YoY — offering a significantly more affordable entry point versus Seoul, where comparable units cost roughly 3x more. Gross rental yields average 4.5%, above the BOK policy rate of 3.00%, providing positive carry for leveraged investors. The Haeundae waterfront commands premium pricing driven by Marine City tower demand. The North Port redevelopment project ($8B, 2025–2035) represents a multi-decade catalyst for the city's transformation. Growing interest from Japanese and Taiwanese investors, combined with BIFF and MICE infrastructure positioning, is driving incremental tourism and residential demand.

釜山是韓國第二大城市及主要港口，人口約340萬。公寓均價約3.5億韓元（約25萬美元），年增+3% — 相較首爾，同類單位價格約為3倍，釜山提供更可負擔的入場點。毛租金收益率平均4.5%，高於韓國銀行3.00%政策利率，為槓桿投資者提供正利差。海雲台濱海區因Marine City塔樓需求而享有溢價定位。北港再開發計劃（80億美元，2025至2035年）是城市轉型的長期催化劑。來自日本及台灣投資者的日益增長興趣，加上BIFF和MICE基礎設施定位，正推動旅遊及住宅需求增長。

PRICE TREND房價走勢

Busan's apartment market has exhibited moderate growth compared to Seoul's dramatic volatility. Prices rose +8% in 2021, driven by spillover demand and low interest rates, before correcting -4% in 2022 as the BOK tightened aggressively. The market has since stabilized, with 2025 showing a gradual recovery. The price gap with Seoul has been widening — Seoul is now 3.4x Busan, up from 2.8x in 2019, reflecting disproportionate capital concentration in the capital. Peripheral satellite cities such as Gimhae and Yangsan offer more affordable alternatives at 60–70% of Busan core prices.

與首爾劇烈波動相比，釜山公寓市場呈現溫和增長。2021年受外溢需求和低利率推動，價格上漲+8%，隨後2022年韓國銀行積極升息導致修正-4%。此後市場趨於穩定，2025年顯示逐步復甦。與首爾的價差持續擴大——首爾現為釜山的3.4倍，高於2019年的2.8倍，反映資本過度集中於首都。衛星城市如金海和梁山提供更可負擔的替代選擇，價格約為釜山核心區的60至70%。

KEY DISTRICTS重點地區

Haeundae is Busan's premier waterfront district, home to Marine City's luxury towers and commanding the highest prices at ₩680M. Centum City is the tech and commerce hub anchored by BEXCO convention center, with strong corporate tenant demand at ₩520M. Nampo/Jung-gu, the historic old downtown, is undergoing gentrification with attractive yields at ₩320M. Gimhae offers the most affordable entry at ₩250M with airport proximity and growing commuter infrastructure.

海雲台是釜山首屈一指的濱海區，擁有Marine City豪華塔樓，以6.8億韓元的最高價格領先。Centum City是以BEXCO會展中心為核心的科技商業樞紐，企業租戶需求強勁，均價5.2億韓元。南浦/中區為歷史舊城區，正經歷都市更新，均價3.2億韓元，收益率具吸引力。金海以2.5億韓元提供最可負擔的入場點，鄰近機場且通勤基礎設施日益完善。

PORT REDEVELOPMENT港口再開發

The North Port redevelopment project is Busan's most transformative urban initiative, with a total investment of $8 billion spanning 2025 to 2035. The plan transforms the aging port facility into a waterfront mixed-use district with 15,000 residential units, commercial space, cultural amenities, and a new cruise terminal. The project is comparable in scope to London's Docklands or Sydney's Darling Harbour redevelopment. It will fundamentally shift Busan's center of gravity, connecting the old downtown of Jung-gu with the modern Haeundae corridor. Early-stage land parcels adjacent to the redevelopment zone have already seen 8–12% price appreciation in anticipation.

北港再開發計劃是釜山最具變革性的城市計劃，總投資80億美元，橫跨2025至2035年。該計劃將老化港口設施改造為濱海複合開發區，包含15,000套住宅單位、商業空間、文化設施和新郵輪碼頭。其規模可與倫敦碼頭區或雪梨達令港再開發相比。這將從根本上改變釜山的重心，連接中區舊城區與現代海雲台走廊。鄰近再開發區的早期土地地塊已因預期而出現8至12%的價格升值。

TOURISM & MICE觀光與會展

The Busan International Film Festival (BIFF) drives global visibility and positions the city as a cultural destination. BEXCO convention center anchors the MICE sector, hosting 200+ events annually. While the 2030 World Expo bid was lost to Riyadh, the infrastructure investment remains — upgraded transit, hotels, and convention capacity. Busan attracts 12M+ tourists annually, with numbers recovering past pre-COVID levels. Short-term rental demand is particularly strong in Haeundae during summer and festival seasons, with premium units achieving 70%+ annual occupancy at rates 30–40% above standard long-term rents.

釜山國際電影節（BIFF）驅動全球知名度，將城市定位為文化目的地。BEXCO會展中心是MICE產業核心，每年舉辦200多場活動。雖然2030世博申辦敗給利雅德，但基礎設施投資仍在——升級的交通、酒店和會展容量。釜山每年吸引超過1,200萬遊客，人次已恢復超越疫前水平。短期租賃需求在夏季和影展季節的海雲台尤為強勁，優質單位年入住率超過70%，租金比標準長租高出30至40%。

RENTAL MARKET租賃市場

Busan offers a gross yield of 4.5% on average, among the best in South Korea. Haeundae 2-bedroom apartments rent for ₩1.2–1.8M/month ($900–$1,350), with Marine City premium units commanding the top end. The jeonse (lump-sum deposit) ratio remains higher than Seoul at 62%, but the monthly rental market is growing steadily as younger tenants and foreign workers shift preferences. Occupancy rates in Haeundae exceed 92%. Foreign tenant demand is driven primarily by Japanese professionals working in Busan's port and manufacturing sectors, creating a reliable non-Korean rental base.

釜山平均毛收益率4.5%，為韓國最佳之一。海雲台兩房公寓月租120至180萬韓元（900至1,350美元），Marine City優質單位居最高。全租（一次性保證金）比率仍高於首爾，達62%，但月租市場隨年輕租戶和外國勞工偏好轉變而穩定增長。海雲台入住率超過92%。外國租戶需求主要來自在釜山港口和製造業工作的日本專業人士，形成可靠的非韓國租戶基礎。

OUTLOOK展望

Base case: Steady +2–3% price growth in 2026–2027, supported by North Port redevelopment momentum and yield advantage over Seoul. Income-focused investors benefit from positive carry above the BOK rate. Upside: Improved KTX connectivity (Seoul–Busan target under 2 hours), continued tourism growth, and North Port Phase 1 completions could push Haeundae above ₩750M. Risk: Population outflow to Seoul and aging demographics (Busan median age 45+) constrain organic demand. If net migration remains negative, suburban districts like Sasang may underperform.

基本情景：2026至2027年穩定+2至3%價格增長，受北港再開發動力和相對首爾的收益優勢支撐。以收益為導向的投資者受益於高於BOK利率的正利差。上行空間：KTX連通性改善（首爾-釜山目標2小時以內）、旅遊持續增長和北港第一期完工可推動海雲台超過7.5億韓元。風險：人口外流至首爾和老齡化人口結構（釜山中位年齡45歲以上）限制有機需求。若淨遷移持續為負，沙上等郊區可能表現不佳。
