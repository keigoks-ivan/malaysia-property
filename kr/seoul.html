EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Seoul is the world's 5th most expensive city by price-to-income ratio at 15x, with average apartment prices at ₩1.18 billion (+5.5% YoY). Gangnam district commands premiums above ₩2.8 billion. The market is recovering from the 2022 correction, driven primarily by supply scarcity — only 25,000 new units were delivered in 2025. The Seoul metropolitan area houses 26 million people, approximately 50% of the nation's population, underpinning structural demand. Foreign buyers account for roughly 8% of transactions. The reconstruction pipeline of 120,000+ units is the key supply catalyst over the next decade.

首爾以15倍房價收入比位列全球第五昂貴城市，平均公寓價格11.8億韓元（年增+5.5%）。江南區售價高達28億韓元以上。市場正從2022年調整中復甦，主要受供給稀缺驅動——2025年僅交付25,000套新單位。首爾都會區擁有2,600萬人口，約佔全國50%，支撐結構性需求。外國買家約佔交易量的8%。12萬套以上的重建管線是未來十年的關鍵供給催化劑。

PRICE TREND房價走勢

Seoul apartment prices doubled between 2015 and 2021, fuelled by ultra-low interest rates, speculative demand, and chronic undersupply. The market corrected ~8% in 2022–2023 as the Bank of Korea hiked rates aggressively. Recovery began in late 2024, with prices now trending upward again. Gangnam consistently trades at 2.5x the Seoul average, while non-Gangnam districts such as Mapo and Yongsan are catching up, driven by urban redevelopment and subway connectivity. The jeonse-to-sale conversion trend has been a secondary driver in the resale market.

首爾公寓價格在2015至2021年間翻倍，受超低利率、投機需求和長期供給不足推動。2022至2023年市場調整約8%，因韓國銀行大幅加息。2024年底開始復甦，價格目前再次上行。江南區持續以首爾平均值2.5倍交易，而麻浦、龍山等非江南區受城市再開發和地鐵連接推動正在追趕。全租轉售趨勢成為二手市場的次要驅動力。

KEY DISTRICTS重點地區

Seoul's property market is sharply stratified by district. Gangnam/Seocho remains the ultra-premium benchmark, commanding ₩2.8 billion+ per apartment with yields of just 1.8%. Yongsan is emerging as the new CBD, with the International Business District redevelopment pushing average prices to ₩1.8 billion. Mapo/Yeongdeungpo offers mid-premium value at ₩1.3 billion with better yields at 2.8%, benefiting from excellent subway access. Nowon/Dobong in the northern suburbs provides the most affordable entry at ₩650 million with yields of 3.5%.

首爾房地產市場按行政區明顯分層。江南/瑞草仍是超高端基準，每套公寓28億韓元以上，收益率僅1.8%。龍山正崛起為新CBD，國際商務區再開發將均價推至18億韓元。麻浦/永登浦以13億韓元提供中高端價值，收益率2.8%較優，受益於優質地鐵網絡。蘆原/道峰北部郊區提供最實惠入場價6.5億韓元，收益率3.5%。

RECONSTRUCTION重建計畫

Seoul's reconstruction pipeline totals 120,000+ units across major projects, representing the single largest supply catalyst through 2032. Key projects include Banpo Xi (35,000 units), Jamsil Jugong (25,000 units), Dunchon (20,000 units), and Apgujeong Hyundai (18,000 units). The timeline spans 2026–2032. Critically, reconstruction creates a temporary supply squeeze: old units are demolished years before new completions are delivered, reducing available inventory in already supply-constrained districts. This dynamic has historically supported prices in reconstruction zones during the transition period.

首爾重建管線合計超過12萬套，橫跨多個大型項目，是2032年前最大的單一供給催化劑。主要項目包括盤浦Xi（35,000套）、蠶室住公（25,000套）、遁村（20,000套）和狎鷗亭現代（18,000套）。時間跨度為2026至2032年。關鍵的是，重建過程造成臨時供給擠壓：舊單位在新建完工前數年即被拆除，減少了本已供給緊張地區的可用庫存。這種動態在過渡期間歷來支撐重建區域的價格。

JEONSE MARKET全租市場

The jeonse (lump-sum deposit lease) system has over ₩1,000 trillion in outstanding deposits, making it one of the world's most unique housing finance mechanisms. The jeonse-to-sale price ratio has declined from 72% in 2019 to 58% in 2025, reflecting structural changes in the market. The 2022 jeonse fraud crisis exposed systemic risks when landlords defaulted on deposit returns, leading to expanded insurance requirements. The ongoing shift from jeonse toward monthly rent (wolse) is gradually improving yield metrics for investors, as monthly rental income becomes a more reliable cash flow stream.

全租（一次性保證金租賃）制度有超過1,000兆韓元的未償保證金，是全球最獨特的住房金融機制之一。全租與售價比率從2019年的72%降至2025年的58%，反映市場結構性變化。2022年全租詐騙危機暴露了系統性風險——房東無法歸還保證金，促使保險要求擴大。從全租向月租（月貰）的持續轉型正逐步改善投資者的收益指標，月租金收入成為更可靠的現金流來源。

RENTAL YIELD租金收益

Seoul's average gross rental yield stands at 2.5%, with significant variation across districts. Gangnam yields just 1.8% due to extreme price premiums, while northern districts like Nowon and Dobong achieve 3.5%. All yields remain below the BOK base rate of 3.0%, implying negative carry for leveraged investors in prime areas. Monthly rent (wolse) is growing at 5–8% per year as the jeonse system gradually declines, providing a tailwind for yield-focused investors in the mid-to-affordable tier.

首爾平均毛租金收益率為2.5%，各區差異顯著。江南因極端價格溢價僅為1.8%，而蘆原、道峰等北部區域可達3.5%。所有收益率均低於韓國銀行基準利率3.0%，意味著槓桿投資者在核心區域面臨負利差。月租金以每年5至8%增長，隨全租制度逐漸減少，為中等及實惠區域的收益導向投資者提供順風。

OUTLOOK展望

Seoul apartment prices are expected to grow 3–5% in 2026–2027, supported by BOK rate cuts and persistent supply scarcity. Reconstruction project launches from 2027 onward may moderate price growth as new supply enters the market. Long-term demographic headwinds — South Korea's fertility rate of 0.72, the world's lowest — pose structural risks, though Seoul's concentration of economic activity partially offsets national population decline. The Gangnam premium is likely to persist due to school district demand, corporate headquarters proximity, and reconstruction-driven quality upgrades.

首爾公寓價格預計在2026至2027年增長3至5%，受韓國銀行降息和持續供給稀缺支撐。2027年起重建項目推出可能隨新供給入市而緩和價格漲幅。長期人口逆風——韓國生育率0.72為全球最低——構成結構性風險，但首爾經濟活動的集中度部分抵消了全國人口下降。江南溢價可能持續，因學區需求、企業總部鄰近和重建驅動的品質升級。
