EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

South Korea's residential market in 2026 is recovering from the 2022–2023 correction triggered by aggressive BOK rate hikes. Seoul apartment prices have rebounded to ₩1.18B average (+5.5% YoY), driven by chronic supply shortage and the unique Jeonse deposit system unwinding. The government's DSR (Debt Service Ratio) cap of 40% continues to constrain leverage. Household debt at 105% of GDP — the highest in Asia — remains a structural vulnerability. Foreign ownership is legally unrestricted but practically concentrated among Chinese buyers (~60% of foreign transactions). The reconstruction pipeline of 120,000+ Seoul units represents the key medium-term supply catalyst.

韓國2026年住宅市場正從2022-2023年韓國銀行激進升息引發的調整中復甦。首爾公寓均價回升至11.8億韓元（年增5.5%），受慢性供應短缺和獨特的傳貰（全租）制度解體推動。政府40%的DSR（債務服務比率）上限繼續限制槓桿。家庭負債佔GDP 105%——亞洲最高——仍是結構性脆弱點。外國人所有權在法律上不受限制，但實際上集中在中國買家（佔外國交易的約60%）。首爾12萬+套重建項目管線代表關鍵的中期供應催化劑。

MACRO CONTEXT & JEONSE SYSTEM宏觀背景與傳貰制度

Korea's unique Jeonse system — where tenants pay a large lump-sum deposit (typically 60–80% of property value) instead of monthly rent — is structurally unwinding. The Jeonse ratio has declined from 72% in 2019 to 58% in 2025 as rising rates made the system less attractive for landlords. This shift creates both risk (Jeonse fraud cases surged in 2022–2023) and opportunity (growing monthly rental market with yields improving). GDP growth of 2.1% is moderate, with semiconductor exports driving the economy. The BOK cut rates to 3.00% in January 2025, the first cut since the hiking cycle.

韓國獨特的傳貰（全租）制度——租戶支付大額保證金（通常為房價的60-80%）代替月租——正在結構性解體。傳貰比率從2019年72%下降至2025年58%，因利率上升使該制度對房東吸引力降低。這種轉變既創造風險（2022-2023年傳貰詐騙案激增）也帶來機會（月租市場增長，收益率改善）。GDP增長2.1%溫和，半導體出口驅動經濟。韓國銀行2025年1月降息至3.00%，為升息週期以來首次。

FOREIGN OWNERSHIP FRAMEWORK外國所有權框架

Foreigners can own Korean real estate without quota restrictions — a notable contrast to most Asian markets. Purchase requires registration with local authorities and a Korean bank account. Capital gains tax applies at 60–70% for non-residents holding less than 2 years, dropping to standard rates after that. Chinese nationals account for ~60% of foreign property purchases, followed by Americans and Japanese. Gangnam and Yongsan districts are the most popular foreign investor targets.

外國人可以不受配額限制地擁有韓國房地產——與大多數亞洲市場形成顯著對比。購買需向地方當局登記並開設韓國銀行帳戶。非居民持有不滿2年的資本利得稅為60-70%，之後降至標準稅率。中國國籍佔外國房產購買的約60%，其次是美國人和日本人。江南和龍山區是最受外國投資者歡迎的目標。

SEOUL OVERVIEW首爾概覽

Seoul accounts for ~50% of Korea's total property value. Gangnam district commands ₩2.8B+ average. The reconstruction pipeline (Banpo, Jamsil, Dunchon) will add 120,000+ units over 2026–2030. Supply constraints are acute — Seoul's geographic limits (mountains, river, DMZ proximity) and strict zoning make new land scarce. The Yongsan International Business District redevelopment is the largest single project.

首爾佔韓國房產總價值的約50%。江南區平均超過28億韓元。重建在建量（盤浦、蠶室、둔촌）將在2026-2030年增加12萬+套。供應限制嚴峻——首爾的地理限制（山脈、漢江、接近DMZ）和嚴格的分區規劃使新土地稀缺。龍山國際商務區重建是最大的單一項目。

BUSAN OVERVIEW釜山概覽

Busan is Korea's second city with a more affordable entry point at ₩350M average. The Haeundae and Marine City areas offer premium waterfront living with yields of 4–5%. Busan is positioning as a MICE and film hub, with the Busan International Film Festival driving tourism demand. Port redevelopment (North Port) is a long-term catalyst. Foreign buyer activity is lower than Seoul but growing among Japanese and Taiwanese investors due to proximity and affordability.

釜山是韓國第二大城市，入場門檻較低，均價3.5億韓元。海雲台和Marine City區域提供優質濱海生活，收益率4-5%。釜山正定位為MICE和電影中心，釜山國際電影節推動旅遊需求。港口重建（北港）是長期催化劑。外國買家活動低於首爾，但因地理接近和價格優勢，日本和台灣投資者日益增加。

POLICY & REGULATION政策與監管

Korea's property policy is among the most intervention-heavy globally. Key measures: DSR 40% cap, comprehensive real estate tax on multi-home owners, 60–70% capital gains tax for short-term holdings, loan restrictions in “overheated zones” (most of Seoul). The current government has modestly relaxed some restrictions to support market recovery. Reconstruction project approvals have accelerated. The Jeonse insurance system was expanded after the 2022 fraud crisis.

韓國的房產政策是全球干預最重的之一。關鍵措施：DSR 40%上限、多房屋所有者綜合房地產稅、短期持有60-70%資本利得稅、「過熱區」（首爾大部分區域）貸款限制。現任政府已適度放寬部分限制以支持市場復甦。重建項目審批已加速。2022年詐騙危機後傳貰保險制度已擴大。

OUTLOOK 2026–2028展望 2026–2028

Seoul prices are forecast to grow 3–5% in 2026–2027, supported by supply constraints and rate cuts. Busan may see 2–3% growth. Key catalysts: BOK rate cuts (expected 2 more in 2026), reconstruction project launches, and growing foreign interest. Risks: household debt overhang, potential policy re-tightening if prices surge, and demographic headwinds (fertility rate 0.72, world's lowest). The Jeonse system's continued evolution will reshape the rental landscape.

首爾房價預計2026-2027年增長3-5%，受供應限制和降息支持。釜山可能增長2-3%。關鍵催化劑：韓國銀行降息（預期2026年再降2次）、重建項目啟動和外國興趣增長。風險：家庭負債壓力、價格飆升時可能重新收緊政策、人口結構逆風（出生率0.72，全球最低）。傳貰制度的持續演變將重塑租賃格局。
