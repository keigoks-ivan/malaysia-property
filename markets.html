GLOBAL MARKET INVESTMENT KNOWLEDGE 全球股市投資知識
How to Read PE Ratio如何解讀本益比（PE）
PE = how many years of earnings you're paying for. <12: cheap or troubled. 12-18: fair value. >20: growth premium or overpriced. Compare within the same market's historical range, not across countries — EM markets structurally trade at lower PE than DM. PE = 你正在為幾年的獲利付費。<12：便宜或有問題。12-18：合理估值。>20：成長溢價或過貴。應與同市場歷史區間比較，而非跨國比較——新興市場結構性地比成熟市場PE低。
PEG: The Growth-Adjusted PEPEG：成長調整後的本益比
PEG = PE ÷ EPS growth rate. Normalizes PE for growth speed. <1.0: undervalued growth (Taiwan, Korea, Vietnam). 1.0-1.5: fairly valued. >2.0: overpaying for growth (NZ, AU, Saudi). Peter Lynch's rule: never buy PEG >2 unless it's a compounding monopoly. PEG = PE ÷ 獲利成長率。用成長速度校正PE。<1.0：成長被低估（台灣、韓國、越南）。1.0-1.5：合理定價。>2.0：為成長付過多溢價（紐西蘭、澳洲、沙烏地）。彼得林區的規則：除非是壟斷型複利機器，否則不買PEG >2。
ERP: Are Stocks Worth the Risk?ERP：股票值得冒險嗎？
Equity Risk Premium = earnings yield − bond yield. If ERP <1%, bonds offer similar return with less risk — stocks are expensive. >4%: stocks very attractive (Korea +6.3%, CSI 300 +5.2%). 2-4%: normal (TOPIX +3.8%, DAX +4.2%). <1%: caution (US +0.6%, NZ +0%). Negative: bonds beat stocks (India -2.0%, Brazil -0.7%). Japan's 10yr surged to 2.43% — its ERP compressed from +5.4% to +3.8%, making bonds more competitive vs TOPIX. 股權風險溢酬 = 益本比 − 債券利率。若ERP <1%，債券以更低風險提供類似報酬——股票偏貴。>4%：股票非常有吸引力（韓國+6.3%、滬深300 +5.2%）。2-4%：正常（東證+3.8%、DAX +4.2%）。<1%：謹慎（美國+0.6%、紐西蘭+0%）。為負：債券勝股票（印度-2.0%、巴西-0.7%）。日本10年期飆升至2.43%——ERP從+5.4%壓縮至+3.8%，債券對東證的競爭力上升。
GDP Growth ≠ Stock ReturnsGDP成長 ≠ 股市報酬
Common trap: buying fast-GDP countries. India has GDP 6.3% but Nifty YTD -3.5% (PE too high). China 4.5% GDP but hit by 34% retaliatory tariffs. Thailand 1.7% GDP (revised from 3%) — political risk kills growth. Vietnam 4.0% GDP (revised from 6%) — still attractive at PEG 0.94. The lesson: earnings growth + valuation matter more than GDP. Korea GDP is only 1.8% but KOSPI is up 15% on 45% EPS growth. 常見陷阱：買GDP高成長的國家。印度GDP 6.3%但Nifty YTD -3.5%（PE太高）。中國GDP 4.5%但被34%報復性關稅打擊。泰國GDP 1.7%（從3%下修）——政治風險殺死成長。越南GDP 4.0%（從6%下修）——PEG 0.94仍有吸引力。教訓：獲利成長+估值比GDP重要。韓國GDP僅1.8%但KOSPI因45% EPS成長漲15%。
PB Ratio: Asset-Heavy MarketsPB：資產密集型市場
PB = price relative to net assets. Most useful for banks, REITs, commodities. PB <1 = market says assets are worth less than book (Korea, HK). PB >3 = paying premium for intangibles (US tech). For tech-heavy indices (NASDAQ), PB is less meaningful — use PE/PEG instead. PB = 股價相對淨資產。最適用於銀行、REIT、大宗商品。PB <1 = 市場認為資產低於帳面價值（韓國、香港）。PB >3 = 為無形資產付溢價（美國科技）。科技權重高的指數（NASDAQ），PB參考價值低——改用PE/PEG。
Currency Risk in Global Investing全球投資的匯率風險
A market returning +10% in local currency but whose currency falls 8% vs USD = only +2% in USD terms. High-yield EM currencies (BRL, MXN, IDR) tend to depreciate over time. JPY is structurally weak under BOJ policy. CHF, SGD are safe-haven currencies. Factor FX into total return calculations. 一個市場本幣報酬+10%但貨幣兌美元貶值8%=美元計價僅+2%。高利率新興市場貨幣（巴西雷亞爾、墨西哥披索、印尼盾）長期趨向貶值。日圓在BOJ政策下結構性偏弱。瑞士法郎、新加坡幣是避險貨幣。計算總報酬時須納入匯率因素。
Value vs Growth Markets價值型 vs 成長型市場
Value markets (low PE, high yield): UK, Korea, HK, Brazil, Spain — cheap but often for good reason (governance, growth, structure). Growth markets (high PE, low yield): US, India, Taiwan — expensive but earnings growth justifies premium. The sweet spot: growth at reasonable price (GARP) = PEG <1.5 + positive EPS momentum. 價值型市場（低PE、高殖利率）：英國、韓國、香港、巴西、西班牙——便宜但通常有原因（治理、成長、結構）。成長型市場（高PE、低殖利率）：美國、印度、台灣——貴但獲利成長支撐溢價。最佳區域：合理價格的成長 (GARP) = PEG <1.5 + EPS動能為正。
Dividend Yield: Income Floor股息殖利率：收入底線
High-dividend markets (SG 4.5%, UK 3.8%, AU 3.8%) act as income floor. But: dividends can be cut. Check payout ratio (earnings coverage) and free cash flow. A 5% yield from a declining earnings base is a value trap. Prefer markets where dividends are growing, not just high. 高配息市場（新加坡4.5%、英國3.8%、澳洲3.8%）充當收入底部。但：股息可能被砍。檢查配息比率（獲利覆蓋率）和自由現金流。獲利下滑基礎上的5%殖利率是價值陷阱。偏好股息正在成長的市場，而非僅僅殖利率高的市場。
Market Cap = Liquidity + Access市值 = 流動性 + 可及性
>$5T: deep liquidity, ETF-accessible (US, CN, JP). $1-5T: good liquidity (EU, IN, TW, KR, AU, CA). <$1T: thinner markets, wider spreads, harder to exit (MY, TH, VN, NZ, PH). Smaller markets can offer alpha but position sizing and exit strategy matter more. >$5T：深度流動性、ETF可投（美、中、日）。$1-5T：良好流動性（歐洲、印度、台灣、韓國、澳洲、加拿大）。<$1T：較淺的市場、較寬的價差、較難退出（馬、泰、越、紐、菲）。小市場可能提供超額報酬但部位大小和退出策略更重要。
Interest Rate Cycle: The Master Variable利率週期：主宰一切的變數
2026 reality: US 10yr at 4.31%, Japan 10yr surged to 2.43% (from 1% in 2024). Fed expected to cut but 15% tariff is inflationary → fewer cuts. BOJ is raising rates → JPY strengthening, squeezing exporters. ECB cutting → European banks/REITs benefit. The playbook: watch the US 2yr-10yr spread: inversion = recession signal. When the Fed pivots, rotate from cash to long-duration (tech, REITs, EM). Most global markets follow the Fed with 3-6 month lag. Key risk: tariffs make inflation sticky → delays rate cuts → stocks stay range-bound. 2026現實：美國10年期4.31%，日本10年期飆升至2.43%（2024年僅1%）。聯準會預計降息但15%關稅具通膨效果→降息次數減少。BOJ在升息→日圓升值壓縮出口商。ECB在降息→歐洲銀行/REIT受惠。操作邏輯：觀察美國2年-10年期利差：倒掛=衰退信號。聯準會轉向時，從現金輪動到長天期（科技、REIT、新興市場）。大多數市場跟隨聯準會3-6個月。關鍵風險：關稅讓通膨黏著→延遲降息→股票區間震盪。
Sector Rotation: Follow the Cycle板塊輪動：跟著景氣走
Early recovery: financials, industrials, small caps (cheap cyclicals). Mid-cycle expansion: tech, consumer discretionary (earnings acceleration). Late cycle: energy, materials, commodities (inflation hedge). Recession: healthcare, utilities, staples (defensive). Global application: US leads tech cycle, Japan leads industrials/autos, AU/Brazil lead commodities, SG/UK lead financials. Don't fight the cycle — position with it. 早期復甦：金融、工業、小型股（便宜的週期股）。中期擴張：科技、非必需消費（獲利加速）。晚期擴張：能源、原物料、大宗商品（通膨避險）。衰退期：醫療、公用事業、必需消費（防禦型）。全球應用：美國領導科技週期、日本領導工業/汽車、澳洲/巴西領導大宗商品、新加坡/英國領導金融。不要逆週期——順勢布局。
Correlation: True Diversification相關性：真正的分散
Holding US + NASDAQ + S&P is not diversification — they're 95% correlated. Real diversification needs low-correlation pairs: US tech + Japan value, growth stocks + commodities, DM equities + frontier markets. Best diversifiers for a US-heavy portfolio: Japan (corporate reform story), Brazil (commodity + high yield), Switzerland (defensive CHF). During crises, all correlations go to 1 — only cash, gold, and US Treasuries truly diversify in a crash. 持有美股+NASDAQ+S&P並不是分散——它們95%相關。真正的分散需要低相關性配對：美國科技+日本價值、成長股+大宗商品、成熟市場+邊境市場。美股重倉組合的最佳分散器：日本（企業改革故事）、巴西（大宗商品+高殖利率）、瑞士（防禦型CHF）。危機期間所有相關性趨向1——只有現金、黃金和美國國債在崩盤時真正分散風險。
EM vs DM: The Discount Is Real, So Is the Risk新興 vs 成熟市場：折價是真的，風險也是
EM trades at 30-50% PE discount to DM — but this isn't free money. The discount reflects: weaker rule of law, currency depreciation, political risk, capital controls, lower corporate governance. The filter: only buy EM when (1) PE discount is wider than historical average, (2) USD is weakening (EM inflow catalyst), (3) commodity cycle is turning up. Best EM risk/reward today: Vietnam (FTSE upgrade catalyst), Korea (governance reform), India (structural growth but expensive). Avoid: markets with capital controls (China A-shares) or political instability (Thailand, Turkey). 新興市場PE比成熟市場折價30-50%——但這不是免費的錢。折價反映：法治較弱、貨幣貶值、政治風險、資本管制、公司治理較差。篩選條件：只在以下情況買進新興市場：(1)PE折價大於歷史平均、(2)美元走弱（新興市場資金流入催化劑）、(3)大宗商品週期轉升。目前最佳新興市場風險/報酬：越南（FTSE升級催化劑）、韓國（治理改革）、印度（結構性成長但偏貴）。避開：有資本管制的市場（中國A股）或政治不穩定的市場（泰國、土耳其）。
Geopolitical Risk: Pricing the Unpriceable地緣政治風險：為不可定價的事物定價
Markets only price known risks. Taiwan Strait: TAIEX trades at PEG <1 partly because of invasion risk — it's the world's cheapest growth market for a reason. Middle East: Tadawul's oil dependency means every conflict is a repricing event. Russia/Ukraine: European energy costs permanently higher → EU equities carry a structural risk premium. The rule: geopolitical risk = don't over-concentrate. No single country should be >30% of your portfolio, no matter how cheap it looks. Diversification is the only free lunch. 市場只為已知風險定價。台灣海峽：台股PEG <1部分是因為入侵風險——這是全球最便宜成長市場的原因。中東：沙烏地的石油依賴意味著每次衝突都是重新定價事件。俄烏戰爭：歐洲能源成本永久性升高→歐股承擔結構性風險溢酬。原則：地緣政治風險=不要過度集中。無論看起來多便宜，單一國家不應超過投資組合的30%。分散是唯一免費的午餐。
Buyback Yield: The Hidden Return回購殖利率：隱藏的報酬
US companies return more cash via buybacks than dividends. Total shareholder yield = dividend yield + buyback yield. S&P 500's dividend yield is only 1.3%, but total yield is ~4.5% when including buybacks. Japan: buybacks surging post-governance reform (Tokyo Stock Exchange pressuring PB <1 companies). Korea: "Korea Discount" narrowing as chaebols increase buybacks. EU/SG: prefer dividends over buybacks. When comparing markets, use total shareholder yield, not just dividend yield. 美國企業透過回購返還的現金比股息更多。股東總報酬率=殖利率+回購殖利率。S&P 500殖利率僅1.3%，但加上回購後總報酬率約4.5%。日本：治理改革後回購激增（東證施壓PB<1的企業）。韓國：「韓國折價」因財閥增加回購而收窄。歐洲/新加坡：偏好股息而非回購。比較市場時，使用股東總報酬率，而非僅看殖利率。
ETF vs Stock Picking: Global EditionETF vs 選股：全球版
Rule of thumb: use ETFs for markets you don't know well, pick stocks in markets you understand deeply. Efficient markets (US, UK, Japan): hard to beat the index → use low-cost ETFs (VTI, VXUS, EWJ). Inefficient markets (Vietnam, Philippines, Malaysia): local knowledge creates alpha → stock picking can beat index by 5-10%. Key ETFs: VT (global all-cap), VWO (EM), EWJ (Japan), EWT (Taiwan), EWY (Korea), VAS (Australia). Cost matters: 0.03% (VTI) vs 0.60% (EM ETF) — the gap compounds to 6% over 10 years. 經驗法則：不熟悉的市場用ETF，深入了解的市場用選股。效率市場（美、英、日）：難以打敗指數→用低成本ETF（VTI、VXUS、EWJ）。非效率市場（越南、菲律賓、馬來西亞）：在地知識創造超額報酬→選股可贏指數5-10%。關鍵ETF：VT（全球全市值）、VWO（新興市場）、EWJ（日本）、EWT（台灣）、EWY（韓國）、VAS（澳洲）。成本重要：0.03%（VTI）vs 0.60%（新興市場ETF）——差距10年複利成6%。
Time in Market > Timing the Market待在市場 > 擇時進出
Missing the 10 best days in a 20-year period cuts your return by more than half. The best days often occur within 2 weeks of the worst days — you can't capture one without enduring the other. Dollar-cost averaging (DCA) into global ETFs is the statistically optimal strategy for 90% of investors. The only valid timing signal: extreme valuations. When CAPE (Shiller PE) is >30 (US today), expect lower 10yr forward returns. When CAPE <15 (parts of EM), expect higher forward returns. But "expect" ≠ "guaranteed" — stay invested, adjust allocation at extremes. 在20年期間錯過最好的10天，報酬就會減少超過一半。最好的幾天往往出現在最差的幾天之後2週內——你無法捕捉一個而逃避另一個。定期定額（DCA）投入全球ETF是90%投資人統計上的最佳策略。唯一有效的擇時信號：極端估值。CAPE（席勒PE）>30（美國目前），預期10年前瞻報酬較低。CAPE <15（部分新興市場），預期前瞻報酬較高。但「預期」≠「保證」——留在市場中，在極端時調整配置。
Concentration Risk: The Silent Killer集中度風險：沉默的殺手
S&P 500: top 10 stocks = 35%+ of index weight. Buying "the index" is actually a concentrated bet on Mag7. TAIEX: TSMC alone = 30%+ of index. KOSPI: Samsung + SK Hynix = 25%+. STI: 3 banks = 50%+. If you hold country ETFs in multiple markets, check if you're accidentally double-counting the same themes (e.g., AI via NASDAQ + Taiwan + Korea = triple-counting semiconductors). The fix: check top-10 holdings of every ETF you own. If the same names appear in 3+ ETFs, you're concentrated, not diversified. S&P 500：前10大股票=指數權重35%+。買「指數」其實是集中押注Mag7。台股：僅台積電=指數30%+。KOSPI：三星+SK海力士=25%+。海峽時報：3家銀行=50%+。如果你持有多國ETF，檢查是否重複計算相同主題（例如AI透過NASDAQ+台灣+韓國=三重計算半導體）。解法：檢查你持有每支ETF的前10大持股。如果相同名字出現在3支以上ETF，你是集中的，不是分散的。
Cross-Border Tax: The Return You Never See跨境稅務：你永遠看不到的報酬
Dividend withholding tax silently eats your return. US stocks: 30% withheld for non-US investors (reduced to 15% with tax treaty). Japan: 15%. Australia: 0% (franking credits). Singapore: 0%. EU varies: 25-30%. Impact: a 3% yield with 30% withholding = effective 2.1%. Over 10 years, that's 9% of total return gone. Solutions: (1) invest via countries with tax treaties, (2) prefer markets with 0% withholding (AU, SG), (3) hold accumulating ETFs (reinvest dividends within the fund, deferring tax). Capital gains tax also varies wildly: 0% (HK, SG, MY) to 20%+ (US, UK, AU). 股息預扣稅悄悄侵蝕你的報酬。美股：非美國投資人預扣30%（有稅務協定降至15%）。日本：15%。澳洲：0%（抵減稅額）。新加坡：0%。歐洲不等：25-30%。影響：3%殖利率被預扣30%=實際2.1%。10年下來，這是總報酬9%的損失。解法：(1)透過有稅務協定的國家投資、(2)偏好0%預扣的市場（澳洲、新加坡）、(3)持有累積型ETF（在基金內再投資股息，遞延稅負）。資本利得稅差異也極大：0%（港、新、馬）到20%+（美、英、澳）。
Home Bias: Your Biggest Mistake母國偏誤：你最大的錯誤
Studies show investors allocate 70-80% to their home market, despite it being only 2-60% of global market cap. A Taiwanese investor 100% in TAIEX has 30% of their wealth in a single company (TSMC) exposed to geopolitical risk. A Malaysian investor 100% in KLCI has 0.3% of global opportunities. The optimal split (academic consensus): 60-70% global, 30-40% home market (for currency matching with living expenses). The easiest fix: add one global ETF (VT or VWRA) as your core holding. 研究顯示投資人將70-80%配置在母國市場，儘管母國可能只佔全球市值的2-60%。台灣投資人100%在台股=30%資產在單一公司（台積電），暴露於地緣政治風險。馬來西亞投資人100%在KLCI=全球機會的0.3%。最佳配比（學術共識）：60-70%全球、30-40%母國（與生活費用的幣別匹配）。最簡單的修正：加一支全球ETF（VT或VWRA）作為核心持倉。
Earnings Quality: Not All EPS Are Equal獲利品質：不是所有EPS都一樣
A market with 15% EPS growth driven by share buybacks (US) is very different from 15% driven by revenue growth (India, Vietnam). Buyback-driven EPS growth flatters per-share metrics but doesn't create new economic value. Check: revenue growth vs EPS growth.
