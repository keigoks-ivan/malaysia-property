GLOBAL MARKET INVESTMENT KNOWLEDGE 全球股市投資知識
How to Read PE Ratio如何解讀本益比（PE）
PE = how many years of earnings you're paying for. <12: cheap or troubled. 12-18: fair value. >20: growth premium or overpriced. Compare within the same market's historical range, not across countries — EM markets structurally trade at lower PE than DM. PE = 你正在為幾年的獲利付費。<12：便宜或有問題。12-18：合理估值。>20：成長溢價或過貴。應與同市場歷史區間比較，而非跨國比較——新興市場結構性地比成熟市場PE低。
PEG: The Growth-Adjusted PEPEG：成長調整後的本益比
PEG = PE ÷ EPS growth rate. Normalizes PE for growth speed. <1.0: undervalued growth (Taiwan, Korea, Vietnam). 1.0-1.5: fairly valued. >2.0: overpaying for growth (NZ, AU, Saudi). Peter Lynch's rule: never buy PEG >2 unless it's a compounding monopoly. PEG = PE ÷ 獲利成長率。用成長速度校正PE。<1.0：成長被低估（台灣、韓國、越南）。1.0-1.5：合理定價。>2.0：為成長付過多溢價（紐西蘭、澳洲、沙烏地）。彼得林區的規則：除非是壟斷型複利機器，否則不買PEG >2。
ERP: Are Stocks Worth the Risk?ERP：股票值得冒險嗎？
Equity Risk Premium = earnings yield − bond yield. If ERP <1%, bonds offer similar return with less risk — stocks are expensive. >4%: stocks very attractive (Japan, Korea, HK). 2-4%: normal. <1%: caution (US, India, NZ). Negative ERP = bonds beat stocks on yield. 股權風險溢酬 = 益本比 − 債券利率。若ERP <1%，債券以更低風險提供類似報酬——股票偏貴。>4%：股票非常有吸引力（日本、韓國、香港）。2-4%：正常。<1%：謹慎（美國、印度、紐西蘭）。ERP為負=債券收益率勝過股票。
GDP Growth ≠ Stock ReturnsGDP成長 ≠ 股市報酬
Common trap: buying fast-GDP countries. China has 5% GDP growth but CSI 300's 5yr return underperformed the US. Why? Growth is already priced in, diluted by new share issuance, or captured by SOEs not listed minority shareholders. Focus on earnings growth + valuation, not headline GDP. 常見陷阱：買GDP高成長的國家。中國GDP成長5%但CSI 300五年報酬落後美國。為何？成長已被定價、被增發股份稀釋、或被國企而非上市小股東捕獲。專注於獲利成長+估值，而非GDP標題數字。
PB Ratio: Asset-Heavy MarketsPB：資產密集型市場
PB = price relative to net assets. Most useful for banks, REITs, commodities. PB <1 = market says assets are worth less than book (Korea, HK). PB >3 = paying premium for intangibles (US tech). For tech-heavy indices (NASDAQ), PB is less meaningful — use PE/PEG instead. PB = 股價相對淨資產。最適用於銀行、REIT、大宗商品。PB <1 = 市場認為資產低於帳面價值（韓國、香港）。PB >3 = 為無形資產付溢價（美國科技）。科技權重高的指數（NASDAQ），PB參考價值低——改用PE/PEG。
Currency Risk in Global Investing全球投資的匯率風險
A market returning +10% in local currency but whose currency falls 8% vs USD = only +2% in USD terms. High-yield EM currencies (BRL, MXN, IDR) tend to depreciate over time. JPY is structurally weak under BOJ policy. CHF, SGD are safe-haven currencies. Factor FX into total return calculations. 一個市場本幣報酬+10%但貨幣兌美元貶值8%=美元計價僅+2%。高利率新興市場貨幣（巴西雷亞爾、墨西哥披索、印尼盾）長期趨向貶值。日圓在BOJ政策下結構性偏弱。瑞士法郎、新加坡幣是避險貨幣。計算總報酬時須納入匯率因素。
Value vs Growth Markets價值型 vs 成長型市場
Value markets (low PE, high yield): UK, Korea, HK, Brazil, Spain — cheap but often for good reason (governance, growth, structure). Growth markets (high PE, low yield): US, India, Taiwan — expensive but earnings growth justifies premium. The sweet spot: growth at reasonable price (GARP) = PEG <1.5 + positive EPS momentum. 價值型市場（低PE、高殖利率）：英國、韓國、香港、巴西、西班牙——便宜但通常有原因（治理、成長、結構）。成長型市場（高PE、低殖利率）：美國、印度、台灣——貴但獲利成長支撐溢價。最佳區域：合理價格的成長 (GARP) = PEG <1.5 + EPS動能為正。
Dividend Yield: Income Floor股息殖利率：收入底線
High-dividend markets (SG 4.5%, UK 3.8%, AU 3.8%) act as income floor. But: dividends can be cut. Check payout ratio (earnings coverage) and free cash flow. A 5% yield from a declining earnings base is a value trap. Prefer markets where dividends are growing, not just high. 高配息市場（新加坡4.5%、英國3.8%、澳洲3.8%）充當收入底部。但：股息可能被砍。檢查配息比率（獲利覆蓋率）和自由現金流。獲利下滑基礎上的5%殖利率是價值陷阱。偏好股息正在成長的市場，而非僅僅殖利率高的市場。
Market Cap = Liquidity + Access市值 = 流動性 + 可及性
>$5T: deep liquidity, ETF-accessible (US, CN, JP). $1-5T: good liquidity (EU, IN, TW, KR, AU, CA). <$1T: thinner markets, wider spreads, harder to exit (MY, TH, VN, NZ, PH). Smaller markets can offer alpha but position sizing and exit strategy matter more. >$5T：深度流動性、ETF可投（美、中、日）。$1-5T：良好流動性（歐洲、印度、台灣、韓國、澳洲、加拿大）。<$1T：較淺的市場、較寬的價差、較難退出（馬、泰、越、紐、菲）。小市場可能提供超額報酬但部位大小和退出策略更重要。
