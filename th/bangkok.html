EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Bangkok's condo market in 2026 operates on two distinct speeds. The CBD luxury segment, anchored by the BTS/MRT transit network and 103,000+ foreign residents, continues to appreciate at +1.6% YoY with prime units at THB 315,000/sqm. Meanwhile, the suburban mass market is weighed down by 235,000 unsold units and mortgage rejection rates exceeding 70% for sub-THB 3M properties. Foreign investors face a compelling risk-return proposition in transit-adjacent CBD condos: 4–6% gross yields, capital appreciation of 1–3% annually, and the structural tailwind of the LTR visa program (7,000+ issued) and One Bangkok mega-project completion.

曼谷2026年的公寓市場以兩種截然不同的速度運作。CBD豪宅板塊以BTS/MRT捷運網絡和103,000多名外籍居民為支撐，繼續以年增1.6%的速度升值，優質單位達泰銖315,000/平方米。然而，郊區大眾市場受到235,000套未售單位和300萬泰銖以下房產超過70%按揭拒貸率的拖累。外國投資者在捷運毗鄰CBD公寓面臨具吸引力的風險回報：4至6%毛租金收益率、年1至3%資本增值，以及LTR簽證計劃（已發放7,000多張）和One Bangkok超大型項目完工的結構性順風。

PRICE TREND房價走勢

Bangkok CBD condo prices have risen approximately +40% since 2018, driven by foreign demand concentration, branded residence premiums, and transit infrastructure expansion. In contrast, suburban prices have appreciated only +10% over the same period. This divergence reflects the two-speed nature of the market: international capital flows into CBD freehold condos within the 49% foreign quota, while domestic buyers — constrained by 90.6% household debt-to-GDP — cannot absorb suburban supply. Midtown corridors along newer MRT extensions occupy the middle ground, tracking approximately +17% since 2018.

曼谷CBD公寓價格自2018年以來上漲約+40%，受外國需求集中、品牌住宅溢價和捷運基礎設施擴張推動。相比之下，郊區價格同期僅升值+10%。這種分化反映了市場的雙速本質：國際資本流入49%外國配額內的CBD永久產權公寓，而國內買家因家庭負債佔GDP 90.6%而無力吸收郊區供給。沿較新MRT延伸線的中城走廊居中，自2018年以來約上漲+17%。

KEY DISTRICTS重點地區

Watthana/Sukhumvit remains Bangkok's prime expat corridor at THB 320K/sqm, anchored by Phrom Phong and Thong Lo BTS stations. Bangrak/Silom is the financial district hub at THB 300K/sqm, benefiting from both corporate tenant demand and the One Bangkok mega-project halo. Chatuchak has emerged as the breakout performer, rising +16.4% since the MRT Blue Line extension completion, with prices at THB 180K/sqm offering a significant discount to core CBD.

Watthana/素坤逸仍是曼谷主要外籍走廊，泰銖320K/平方米，以Phrom Phong和Thong Lo BTS站為錨。Bangrak/是隆是金融區核心，泰銖300K/平方米，受益於企業租戶需求和One Bangkok超大型項目光環。恰圖恰成為突破性表現者，自MRT藍線延伸完工以來上漲+16.4%，泰銖180K/平方米的價格相對核心CBD有顯著折讓。

MASS TRANSIT EFFECT大眾捷運效應

The Orange Line corridor has delivered the strongest transit uplift at +18%, followed by the Blue Line Extension at +15%. The Gold Line monorail connecting ICON Siam recorded +12%. Properties within 300 metres of stations command a structural 6–7% premium. The One Bangkok mega-project (THB 120 billion, 16 buildings on Wireless Road) is the single largest catalyst for the Lumphini–Pathum Wan corridor, expected to add 5,000+ residential units and reshape premium pricing benchmarks through 2028.

橙線走廊帶來最強捷運提升效應達+18%，其次是藍線延伸+15%。連接ICON Siam的金線單軌錄得+12%。站點300米範圍內房產有結構性6至7%溢價。One Bangkok超大型項目（1,200億泰銖，Wireless Road上16棟建築）是倫披尼-巴吞旺走廊最大單一催化劑，預計增加5,000多個住宅單位，並在2028年前重塑優質定價基準。

INVENTORY庫存積壓

Bangkok's unsold condo inventory peaked at 235,000 units in 2025, a direct consequence of the domestic demand collapse. Developers have responded by cutting new launches by approximately 40% from 2022 peaks, with 2025E new supply at only 18,000 units versus 60,000 in 2022. This supply discipline is the primary reason CBD prices have held: the luxury segment is not oversupplied. The overhang is concentrated in suburban mass-market projects priced below THB 3 million, where mortgage rejections make absorption structurally difficult.

曼谷未售公寓庫存在2025年達到235,000套的峰值，這是國內需求崩潰的直接結果。開發商已從2022年高峰削減約40%的新推盤，2025年預計新供給僅18,000套，而2022年為60,000套。這種供給紀律是CBD價格維持的主要原因：豪宅板塊並未供過於求。積壓集中在定價低於300萬泰銖的郊區大眾市場項目，按揭拒貸使消化在結構上困難。

RENTAL MARKET租賃市場

Bangkok's CBD rental market delivers 4–6% gross yields for transit-adjacent condos, with net yields of 3–4% after management fees, vacancy, and withholding tax. Airbnb-eligible units in tourist corridors can achieve 7.5% gross during high season, though regulatory tightening on short-term rentals is an ongoing risk. Branded residences (Ritz-Carlton, Four Seasons, Mandarin Oriental) command 25–35% price premiums and attract long-term corporate tenants with higher occupancy stability.

曼谷CBD租賃市場為捷運毗鄰公寓提供4至6%毛租金收益率，扣除管理費、空置率和預扣稅後淨收益率3至4%。旅遊走廊的Airbnb合規單位在旺季可達7.5%毛收益，但短期租賃監管收緊是持續風險。品牌住宅（麗思卡爾頓、四季、文華東方）溢價25至35%，吸引長期企業租戶，入住率更穩定。

OUTLOOK展望

Base case: CBD prime condos appreciate +1–3%/yr through 2028, supported by transit expansion and LTR visa inflows but constrained by global rate environment and 49% quota limitations. Bull case: One Bangkok completion (2025–2027) and accelerated LTR visa uptake drive CBD to THB 385K/sqm by 2029, with Chatuchak and Sukhumvit extensions as secondary beneficiaries. Bear case: Chinese capital retreat (geopolitical/domestic property crisis spillover) reduces foreign demand by 20–30%, pushing CBD prices down to THB 305–310K/sqm before stabilizing.

基本情景：CBD優質公寓在2028年前每年升值+1至3%，受捷運擴張和LTR簽證流入支撐，但受全球利率環境和49%配額限制約束。牛市情景：One Bangkok完工（2025至2027年）和LTR簽證加速推動CBD至2029年泰銖385K/平方米，恰圖恰和素坤逸延伸線為次要受益者。熊市情景：中國資本撤退（地緣政治/國內房地產危機溢出）減少外國需求20至30%，推動CBD價格下跌至泰銖305至310K/平方米後企穩。
