紐西蘭住宅房地產市場報告

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

New Zealand's residential property market is navigating a turning point in early 2026. The RBNZ has delivered 225 basis points of cumulative OCR cuts since August 2024, bringing the Official Cash Rate to 3.25%, with markets pricing a terminal rate of 2.75% by mid-2026. Despite aggressive monetary easing, the national median house price of NZ$820,000 remains 15% below the November 2021 peak of NZ$965,000. A two-speed dynamic has emerged: Christchurch leads the recovery at just $4,000 below its peak with yields of 4.8%, while Auckland languishes with 31 weeks of inventory and prices still 24.9% below their 2022 high. National rental yields of 4.2% produce a spread of +0.95% over the OCR — the most attractive carry position since 2021. However, ANZ has flagged the risk of an OCR hike by December 2026 if inflation surprises to the upside, introducing policy uncertainty into an otherwise improving outlook.

紐西蘭住宅房市在2026年初正經歷轉折點。紐西蘭儲備銀行（RBNZ）自2024年8月以來已累計降息225個基點，將官方現金利率（OCR）降至3.25%，市場預期終端利率將於2026年中達到2.75%。儘管貨幣政策大幅寬鬆，全國房屋中位價NZ$820,000仍較2021年11月高峰NZ$965,000低15%。市場呈現雙速格局：基督城以僅差$4,000即達高峰及4.8%收益率領先復甦，而奧克蘭則以31週庫存及房價仍較2022年高點低24.9%而表現疲弱。全國租金收益率4.2%與OCR之間的利差為+0.95%——為2021年以來最具吸引力的持有收益。然而，ANZ已警示若通膨意外上行，2026年12月前可能重啟加息，為本來改善中的前景增添政策不確定性。

RATE CUTTING CYCLE降息週期

The Reserve Bank of New Zealand embarked on one of the most aggressive easing cycles among developed economies, cutting the OCR by 225 basis points from 5.50% in August 2024 to 3.25% by February 2026. This included two consecutive 50-basis-point cuts in October and November 2024, signaling the RBNZ's urgency to support an economy that had fallen into technical recession. The transmission to mortgage rates has been meaningful: the average two-year fixed rate has dropped from 7.0% to approximately 5.2%, improving borrowing capacity by roughly NZ$80,000–100,000 for the median household.

Market pricing implies a further 50 basis points of cuts, targeting a terminal rate of 2.75% by mid-2026. However, this benign outlook carries an important caveat: ANZ, New Zealand's largest bank, has publicly forecasted the possibility of an OCR hike back to 3.50% or higher by December 2026 if non-tradeable inflation remains sticky above 4%. This creates an asymmetric risk environment — the base case is supportive, but the tail risk of policy reversal could catch over-leveraged borrowers off guard.

紐西蘭儲備銀行展開了已開發經濟體中最積極的寬鬆週期之一，將OCR從2024年8月的5.50%降至2026年2月的3.25%，累計降息225個基點。其中包括2024年10月和11月連續兩次50個基點的降息，顯示RBNZ支撐已陷入技術性衰退之經濟的急迫性。利率傳導效果顯著：兩年期固定房貸平均利率從7.0%降至約5.2%，中位家庭借款能力提升約NZ$8萬至10萬。

市場定價隱含再降50個基點，目標終端利率為2026年中的2.75%。然而這一樂觀前景附帶重要但書：紐西蘭最大銀行ANZ已公開預測，若非貿易財通膨持續黏著在4%以上，2026年12月前OCR可能重新升至3.50%或更高。這創造了不對稱的風險環境——基本情境利好，但政策逆轉的尾部風險可能令過度槓桿的借款人措手不及。

SUPPLY供給面

New Zealand's building consent pipeline has weakened significantly. Annual residential consents fell to approximately 32,000 in 2025, down from the peak of 51,000 in 2022. The decline reflects developer caution amid elevated construction costs, tighter lending conditions, and uncertainty about demand. Multi-unit consents, which had driven the 2021–2022 boom, have fallen 40% from their peak as apartment developers face viability challenges with current build costs exceeding NZ$5,000/sqm in Auckland.

The supply pipeline contraction is a medium-term positive for existing homeowners and investors. With population growth driven by record net migration of 135,000 in 2023 (moderating to an estimated 80,000 in 2025), the dwelling deficit continues to widen. Auckland alone requires an estimated 12,000–15,000 new dwellings annually but is tracking below 10,000 consents. The supply-demand imbalance will eventually translate to renewed price pressure, particularly once interest rates stabilize at lower levels and buyer confidence returns.

紐西蘭建築許可管線明顯走弱。2025年年度住宅許可降至約32,000份，遠低於2022年高峰的51,000份。下降反映開發商在建設成本高企、貸款條件收緊及需求不確定性下的謹慎態度。曾推動2021至2022年建設潮的多戶型許可較高峰下降40%，因公寓開發商在奧克蘭當前建設成本超過NZ$5,000/平方米的環境下面臨可行性挑戰。

供給管線萎縮對現有業主和投資者是中期利好。在2023年創紀錄的135,000淨移民（2025年估計放緩至80,000）推動人口增長的背景下，住宅缺口持續擴大。僅奧克蘭每年就需約12,000至15,000套新住宅，但許可量追蹤在10,000套以下。供需失衡最終將轉化為新的價格壓力，特別是在利率穩定在較低水準且買家信心回歸之後。

DEMAND需求面

Sales volumes are recovering from their 2023 nadir. REINZ reported approximately 5,800 residential sales per month in late 2025, up from the cyclical low of 4,200 in mid-2023 but still well below the 2021 peak of 9,500 monthly transactions. First-home buyers have been the most active segment, supported by lower deposit requirements and improved borrowing capacity from OCR cuts. Investor activity remains subdued relative to historical norms, constrained by the removal of interest deductibility (partially restored to 80% from April 2025) and extended bright-line test periods.

The rental market is showing signs of easing for the first time in several years. National median weekly rents have plateaued at approximately NZ$600, with Auckland rents declining 2–3% year-on-year as elevated inventory provides tenants with more choice. Christchurch rents remain firm at NZ$520/week with yields of 4.8%. The rental market softening is a headwind for investors seeking income, but the improving yield spread over the OCR partially compensates.

銷售量正從2023年低谷回升。REINZ報告2025年下半年月均住宅銷售約5,800套，高於2023年中的週期低點4,200套，但仍遠低於2021年月均9,500套的高峰。首購族是最活躍的群體，受益於較低的首付要求及降息帶來的借貸能力改善。投資者活動相對歷史常態仍偏弱，受限於利息抵扣取消（自2025年4月部分恢復至80%）及延長的明線測試期。

租賃市場數年來首次出現緩和跡象。全國週租中位數在約NZ$600趨穩，奧克蘭租金年減2至3%，因高庫存為租戶提供更多選擇。基督城租金維持穩健在NZ$520/週，收益率4.8%。租賃市場軟化對追求收入的投資者構成逆風，但與OCR之間改善的利差部分抵消了這一影響。

VALUATION估值

New Zealand's national rental yield of 4.2% against the OCR of 3.25% produces a spread of +0.95% — the widest positive spread since 2021 and a meaningful improvement from the -1.3% negative spread that prevailed when the OCR peaked at 5.50%. Christchurch offers the most attractive carry at 4.8% yield, with suburban pockets like Riccarton and Shirley delivering 5.5–5.8%. Auckland's yield of 3.96% provides marginal positive carry over the OCR but leaves little buffer against rate surprises.

The national price-to-income ratio remains elevated at 7.5x, though this has improved from the peak of 9.5x in late 2021. Auckland at 9.2x remains severely unaffordable by international standards, while Christchurch at 5.8x is broadly in line with long-run New Zealand averages. The peak discount analysis shows Christchurch virtually at par (-0.6%), while Auckland carries the deepest discount at -24.9% — suggesting either significant upside potential or a structural repricing to lower equilibrium levels.

紐西蘭全國租金收益率4.2%扣除OCR 3.25%後，利差為+0.95%——為2021年以來最寬的正利差，較OCR高峰5.50%時-1.3%的負利差大幅改善。基督城以4.8%收益率提供最具吸引力的持有收益，瑞卡頓和雪利等郊區更達5.5至5.8%。奧克蘭收益率3.96%對OCR僅有微弱正利差，對利率意外幾無緩衝。

全國房價對收入比仍偏高在7.5倍，但已從2021年末高峰的9.5倍改善。奧克蘭9.2倍以國際標準仍屬嚴重不可負擔，而基督城5.8倍大致符合紐西蘭長期平均。峰值折價分析顯示基督城幾乎持平（-0.6%），而奧克蘭承擔最深折價-24.9%——暗示要麼具有顯著上行潛力，要麼正經歷向更低均衡水準的結構性重新定價。

AUCKLAND奧克蘭

Auckland's median house price of NZ$1,014,000 remains stubbornly below its 2022 peak of NZ$1.35M, a correction of 24.9% that ranks among the deepest in the developed world. The inventory overhang is the primary headwind: at 31 weeks of supply, Auckland is firmly in buyer's market territory, with sellers facing extended listing times averaging 45 days and increasing price negotiation pressure. The correction has been concentrated in the premium suburbs — Herne Bay, Orakei, and Remuera have seen declines of 25–30% from peak, while South and West Auckland have proven more resilient at 15–20% off peak.

The path to recovery in Auckland hinges on inventory normalization. Historical analysis suggests that a buyer's market transitions to balanced at approximately 20 weeks of inventory and to a seller's market below 15 weeks. At the current absorption rate, Auckland is unlikely to reach balanced conditions before late 2026 at the earliest. The silver lining is yield improvement: as prices have fallen while rents held relatively steady, Auckland's gross yield has risen to 3.96%, providing marginally positive carry over the OCR for the first time since 2021.

奧克蘭房屋中位價NZ$1,014,000頑固地低於2022年高峰NZ$1.35M，修正幅度達24.9%，為已開發國家中最深之列。庫存積壓是主要逆風：31週的供給量令奧克蘭穩居買方市場，賣方面臨平均45天的延長掛牌時間及日益增大的議價壓力。修正集中在高端區域——赫恩灣、奧拉凱和雷穆拉較高峰下跌25至30%，而南區和西區奧克蘭較為堅韌，僅較高峰低15至20%。

奧克蘭的復甦路徑取決於庫存正常化。歷史分析顯示，買方市場在庫存約20週時轉為平衡，低於15週時轉為賣方市場。按當前去化速度，奧克蘭最早要到2026年末才能達到平衡狀態。一線曙光是收益率改善：房價下跌而租金相對持穩，奧克蘭毛收益率已升至3.96%，自2021年以來首次對OCR產生微弱正利差。

CHRISTCHURCH基督城

Christchurch is New Zealand's standout performer, with its median house price of NZ$697,000 sitting just $4,000 below the 2022 peak of NZ$701,000 — a recovery rate that no other major New Zealand city has matched. The post-earthquake rebuild, now effectively complete, has left Christchurch with one of the youngest and most energy-efficient housing stocks in the country. This modern stock commands a premium from buyers who value low-maintenance living and lower insurance costs.

The fundamental value proposition is compelling. At 31% below Auckland's median price, Christchurch offers comparable liveability with significantly better affordability. Average gross yields of 4.8% and suburban yields of 5–6% in areas like Riccarton, Addington, and Shirley provide the best carry trade in the country. The University of Canterbury drives rental demand in the inner suburbs, while new family-oriented developments in Halswell and Rolleston are absorbing domestic migration. The risk to Christchurch is its relative lack of economic diversification compared to Auckland, though the city's growing tech sector and established agricultural services base provide some offset.

基督城是紐西蘭表現最突出的市場，房屋中位價NZ$697,000僅比2022年高峰NZ$701,000低$4,000——復甦速度為紐西蘭各主要城市之最。震後重建現已基本完成，為基督城留下全國最年輕、最節能的住宅存量之一。這些現代化住宅因低維護成本和較低保險費用而受到買家青睞。

基本面價值主張極具說服力。較奧克蘭中位價低31%的情況下，基督城提供相當的宜居性和顯著更好的可負擔性。平均毛收益率4.8%，瑞卡頓、艾丁頓和雪利等區域的郊區收益率5至6%，提供全國最佳的套利交易。坎特伯雷大學推動內城區租賃需求，而霍斯威爾和羅爾斯頓面向家庭的新開發項目正在吸收國內遷移。基督城的風險在於相比奧克蘭經濟多元化程度較低，不過該市不斷成長的科技產業和成熟的農業服務基礎提供了部分抵消。

OUTLOOK展望

Base case for 2026: +3–5% nationally. Christchurch to outperform. Auckland remains range-bound until inventory normalizes below 20 weeks. Key risk: ANZ forecasts OCR hike by December 2026 if inflation surprises.

2026年基本情境：全國上漲3至5%。基督城將跑贏大盤。奧克蘭在庫存正常化至20週以下之前將維持區間震盪。主要風險：ANZ預測若通膨意外上行，2026年12月前可能重啟加息。
