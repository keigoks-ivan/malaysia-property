COLUMN LEGEND — what each score measures 欄位說明 — 每個分數量什麼
  • 🛂 Visa — Difficulty of obtaining a long-term (2yr+) visa. Score is the inverse of the minimum capital requirement across available programs: ≤$50K → 95, ≤$200K → 85, ≤$500K → 75, ≤$1M → 65, ≤$2.5M → 50, >$2.5M → 35. Visa goal (PR / passport) then adjusts: fast path → +10, no path → up to -35. 100 if origin = market (no visa needed); 15-25 if all programs closed (e.g. IE Tier 1).
  • 🗣️ Lang — Daily-life language friction. Combines market's English usability + Mandarin speaker % + your selected languages. 100 if origin = market (native speaker assumed). EF EPI 2024 source.
  • ✈️ Flight — Direct flight hours from your origin. ≤2h → 98, ≤4h → 90, ≤8h → 65, >18h → 18. 100 if origin = market.
  • 🌡️ Climate — Temperature comfort + 5 disaster types averaged (earthquake/typhoon/flood/wildfire/haze). Sweet spot 22-27°C. Age 50+ penalised for >28°C. World Bank Climate / UN INDR sources.
  • 🏥 Health — Numbeo Healthcare Index 2025 + bonus for universal coverage. Age 50+ amplifies high/low scores by ±5. WHO + Numbeo sources.
  • 🎓 Edu — Without kids: weighted by QS top university rank. With kids: international school count + curricula diversity (IB/British/American) + tuition affordability. ISC Research + QS 2025 sources.
  • 🛡️ Safety — Numbeo Safety Index 2025 + Global Peace Index rank bonus/penalty. ≤Top 5 GPI → +8, ≥100th → -8.
  • 💰 Tax — Critical for buyers with overseas passive income. Territorial regime (HK/SG/MY) + overseas income exempt → 95. Worldwide regime (US/CA/AU) + has overseas income → 30. US/CA/AU origin gets -10 unless looking at home market (no double-tax). Top marginal rate >45% additional -12. PwC/KPMG/EY tax summaries 2025.
  • Total — Weighted sum across all 8 dimensions, normalised 0-100. Adjust the weight sliders on the left to reflect your priorities. If visa goal is "holiday only", visa weight is forced to 0 (irrelevant).
  • 🛂 簽證 — 取得長期（2 年以上）簽證的難易度。分數為「最低資金門檻」的反函數：≤$5 萬 → 95、≤$20 萬 → 85、≤$50 萬 → 75、≤$100 萬 → 65、≤$250 萬 → 50、>$250 萬 → 35。簽證目標（PR / 入籍）再調整：路徑快 → +10、無路徑 → 最多 -35。原籍 = 該市場 → 100（不需簽證）；所有計畫關閉（如 IE Tier 1）→ 15-25。
  • 🗣️ 語言 — 日常生活語言摩擦。結合市場英語使用度 + 華人比例 + 你勾選的語言。原籍 = 該市場 → 100（假定母語）。資料源 EF EPI 2024。
  • ✈️ 飛行 — 從你原籍直飛時數。≤2h → 98、≤4h → 90、≤8h → 65、>18h → 18。原籍 = 該市場 → 100。
  • 🌡️ 氣候 — 溫度舒適度 + 5 種天災平均（地震 / 颱風 / 洪水 / 野火 / 霾害）。最佳區間 22-27°C。50+ 對 >28°C 額外扣分。資料源 World Bank Climate / UN INDR。
  • 🏥 醫療 — Numbeo Healthcare Index 2025 + 全民健保加分。50+ 對高低分擴大 ±5。資料源 WHO + Numbeo。
  • 🎓 教育 — 無小孩：依 QS 頂尖大學排名加權。有小孩：國際學校數 + 課程多樣性（IB / 英式 / 美式）+ 學費可負擔度。資料源 ISC Research + QS 2025。
  • 🛡️ 治安 — Numbeo Safety Index 2025 + Global Peace Index 排名加減分。GPI 前 5 名 → +8、≥100 名 → -8。
  • 💰 稅務 — 對有海外被動收入買家最關鍵。領土制（HK/SG/MY）+ 海外所得免稅 → 95。全球課稅制（US/CA/AU）+ 有海外收入 → 30。US/CA/AU 原籍額外 -10，**但看自己市場時不扣**（無雙重課稅問題）。最高邊際稅率 >45% 再 -12。資料源 PwC/KPMG/EY 2025 稅務摘要。
  • 總分 — 8 維加權平均 normalised 0-100。調整左方權重滑桿反映你的優先順序。簽證目標若為「純度假」，簽證權重強制歸零（不重要）。
