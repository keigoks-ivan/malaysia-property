Valuation Framework估值框架

Price-to-Income房價所得比

National median multiple of 9.89x far exceeds the 5.1x "severely unaffordable" threshold. Taipei at 15.41x is among the most stretched globally, requiring over 15 years of household income to purchase a median home.全國房價所得比 9.89 倍遠超「嚴重不可負擔」的 5.1 倍門檻。台北 15.41 倍為全球最高水準之一，需超過 15 年家庭所得才能購買中位數住宅。

Price-to-Rent房價租金比

Taipei's P/R ratio of 47x implies a gross yield of ~2.1%. Among APAC cities, only Hong Kong and Shanghai are more expensive on this metric. Rental income alone cannot justify current prices.台北租金比 47 倍意味著毛租金回報率約 2.1%。亞太城市中僅香港和上海更貴。單靠租金收入無法支撐當前房價。

Yield Spread利差分析

At +0.11%, the spread between rental yield and CBS rate is negligible. Any further rate increase would push spreads negative, meaning investors would lose money on a leveraged rental basis. Capital appreciation is the sole return driver.利差僅 +0.11%，租金回報與央行利率之間的差距微乎其微。任何進一步升息都將導致負利差，表示槓桿租賃投資將虧損。資本增值是唯一回報驅動力。
