SUMMARY總結

Price Correction價格修正

Taipei -2.1% — policy-driven correction, not a demand collapse. Credit tightening measures are cooling speculative activity.台北 -2.1% — 政策驅動的修正，非需求崩潰。信用緊縮措施正在冷卻投機活動。

Rental Bright Spot租賃亮點

Rents continue rising steadily, with yields expanding as prices soften. Landlords benefit from tight rental supply.租金持續穩定上漲，收益率因房價軟化而擴大。房東受惠於租賃供給吃緊。

Zone 2 Best Value第二區最佳價值

Neihu/Nangang tech corridor offers the best risk-adjusted value with strong employment anchors and improving infrastructure.內湖／南港科技走廊提供最佳風險調整價值，擁有強勁就業支撐及持續改善的基礎建設。

Zone 1 Caution第一區警示

Xinyi luxury segment faces credit controls and weaker demand from high-net-worth buyers. Premium compression expected.信義豪宅市場面臨信用管控及高淨值買家需求減弱。預期溢價將壓縮。
