Key Cycle Events Timeline 關鍵週期事件時間軸
  • 🟡 Jul 2016: House & Land Combined Tax (房地合一稅) implemented → market cooling, transaction volume shrinks 🟡 2016年7月：房地合一稅實施 → 市場冷卻，交易量萎縮
  • 🟢 Mar 2020: CBC cuts rate to 1.125% (COVID response) → liquidity surge, housing prices accelerate 🟢 2020年3月：CBC 降息至 1.125%（COVID response）→ 流動性湧入，房價加速
  • 🟠 2021: New building permits surge +13.8%, HPI up +13% YoY 🟠 2021年：新建執照衝高 +13.8%，房價年增 13%
  • 🔴 Mar 2022: CBC's first rate hike in 11 years → rate hike cycle begins (4 hikes in 2022) 🔴 2022年3月：CBC 11 年來首次升息 → 升息週期開始（4次 in 2022）
  • 🔴 2022: 6th round of Selective Credit Controls → bank mortgage lending constrained 🔴 2022年：選擇性信用管制升級（第6次）→ 銀行房貸受限
  • 🟡 2023–2025: Volume shrinks, prices rise — transactions at historic lows, HPI still climbing 🟡 2023–2025年：量縮價漲，成交量歷史低位，房價指數仍升
