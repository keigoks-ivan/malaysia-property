Common Pitfalls常見陷阱
⚠ High yield ≠ safe. Manila 6.5% yield comes with condo oversupply and peso depreciation risk. Always check the Supply and Pipeline columns.高收益率 ≠ 安全。馬尼拉 6.5% 收益率伴隨公寓過剩和披索貶值風險。務必查看供需和未來供給欄。
💰 Currency eats returns. A 10% local currency depreciation wipes out 2 years of rental income. Factor FX into total return for any non-home-currency investment.匯率吃掉報酬。當地貨幣貶值10%等於抹去2年租金收入。非本幣投資務必將匯率納入總報酬計算。
🏠 Foreign buyer taxes vary wildly. Singapore adds 60% stamp duty. Australia 8%. Malaysia 8%. Ireland 0%. Canada banned foreign buyers until 2027. This changes your effective entry cost by 0–60%.外國人購房稅差異巨大。新加坡額外60%印花稅。澳洲8%。馬來西亞8%。愛爾蘭0%。加拿大禁止外國買家至2027年。這使你的實際進場成本差異達0–60%。
📈 Negative carry ≠ bad investment. Sydney at -3.1% carry has gained +85% in 5 years. The key: structural supply scarcity. If a city can't build, prices go up regardless of carry.負利差 ≠ 壞投資。雪梨利差 -3.1% 但5年漲了+85%。關鍵：結構性供給稀缺。一座城市蓋不出房子，不管利差正負房價都會漲。

Currency & FX Risk匯率與外匯風險
Rule of thumb: A 10% depreciation in property currency wipes out ~2 years of rental income. Your real return = property return + FX return. 經驗法則：當地貨幣貶值10%等於抹去約2年租金收入。你的實際報酬 = 房產報酬 + 匯率報酬。
▲ Strong currency markets (CHF, SGD, USD): FX tailwind for foreign owners. Currency acts as a bonus return layer.強勢貨幣市場（瑞郎、新幣、美元）：匯率為外國業主帶來順風。貨幣本身是額外報酬層。
▼ Weak/volatile currencies (TRY, IDR, PHP, MYR): High yields can be entirely offset by depreciation. Istanbul 25% YoY growth means nothing if lira drops 30%.弱勢/波動貨幣（土耳其里拉、印尼盾、披索、馬幣）：高收益率可能完全被貶值抵消。伊斯坦堡年增25%在里拉跌30%後毫無意義。
▶ Hedging options: USD-pegged markets (Dubai AED, HK HKD) eliminate FX risk vs USD. JPY weakness has been a structural entry discount for Asian buyers since 2022.對沖選項：掛鉤美元市場（杜拜迪拉姆、港幣）消除對美元匯率風險。日圓弱勢自2022年起為亞洲買家提供結構性進場折價。
Vacancy & Tenant Risk空租與租客風險
Advertised yield assumes 100% occupancy. Actual yield = gross yield × occupancy rate. A 6% yield at 80% occupancy = 4.8% real yield. 廣告收益率假設100%出租。實際收益率 = 毛收益率 × 出租率。6%收益率在80%出租率下 = 實際4.8%。
Low risk低風險Corporate tenants, embassy staff, hospital workers. Vacancy <1%. (Dublin, Tokyo, Singapore)企業租客、使館人員、醫護人員。空置率<1%。（都柏林、東京、新加坡）
Medium中風險Young professionals, students. Seasonal gaps. Vacancy 1-3%. (Melbourne, Taipei, London)年輕專業人士、學生。季節性空窗。空置率1-3%。（墨爾本、台北、倫敦）
High risk高風險Tourist/Airbnb dependent, oversupplied condos. Vacancy 5%+. (Bangkok, Manila, KL serviced apt)依賴觀光客/Airbnb、過剩公寓。空置率5%+。（曼谷、馬尼拉、KL服務式公寓）
Always ask: Who is the tenant? How stable is their income? Can they leave easily?永遠要問：租客是誰？收入穩定嗎？他們容易搬走嗎？

Total Return Formula總報酬公式
True Return =
Capital gain (local currency)
+ Cumulative net rental income
− Entry costs (stamp duty + legal)
− Exit costs (CGT + agent + legal)
− Holding costs (tax + management + vacancy)
± FX gain/loss on total invested capital

Most investors only calculate the first line. Professionals calculate all seven. 真實報酬 =
資本增值（當地貨幣）
+ 累計淨租金收入
− 進場成本（印花稅+律師費）
− 退場成本（資本利得稅+仲介+律師）
− 持有成本（稅+管理+空租）

多數投資人只算第一項。專業投資人算完全部七項。
