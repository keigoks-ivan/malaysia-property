How to Read REIT Metrics如何解讀REIT指標
  • P/NAV — Price to Net Asset Value. Below 1.0 means the market prices the REIT at a discount to its property holdings. Deep discounts (<0.7) may signal value or distress. Premiums (>1.5) reflect growth expectations.P/NAV — 市價淨值比。低於1.0表示市場以折價定價REIT的不動產資產。深度折價(<0.7)可能是價值或困境信號。溢價(>1.5)反映成長預期。
  • Yield vs Growth — High-yield REITs (5%+) typically have lower growth. Growth REITs like data centers may yield only 1-3% but deliver capital gains. Total return = yield + price appreciation.殖利率 vs 成長 — 高殖利率REITs(5%+)通常成長較低。資料中心等成長型REIT可能僅殖利1-3%但帶來資本利得。總報酬=殖利+價格增值。
  • Gearing — Debt-to-assets ratio. Below 25% is conservative. Above 45% is elevated risk, especially in rising rate environments. SG REITs are capped at 50% by regulation.槓桿比率 — 債務資產比。低於25%為保守。超過45%風險較高，尤其在升息環境。新加坡REIT法規上限50%。
Sector Outlook 20262026年產業展望
  • Industrial / Data Center — Strong demand from AI infrastructure buildout and e-commerce logistics. Prologis and Equinix remain sector leaders. Expect 5-8% rental growth.工業/資料中心 — AI基礎建設和電商物流需求強勁。Prologis和Equinix仍為產業領導者。預期租金成長5-8%。
  • Office — Under pressure from hybrid work. CBD Grade-A recovering slowly. Japan and SG offices more resilient than US/EU. Deep discounts on some office REITs.辦公 — 混合辦公模式持續壓力。CBD甲級寫字樓緩慢復甦。日本和新加坡辦公比美歐更有韌性。
  • Retail — Mixed recovery. Prime malls with experiential tenants doing well. Suburban and secondary malls still struggling. Grocery-anchored stable.零售 — 複甦不一。高端體驗型商場表現良好。郊區和次級商場仍在掙扎。生鮮超市為主的穩定。
  • Residential — Steady demand in supply-constrained markets. SFR REITs benefit from housing unaffordability. Multifamily faces oversupply in some US Sun Belt cities.住宅 — 供給受限市場需求穩定。單戶出租REIT受益於購房難。部分美國陽光帶城市多戶住宅面臨過剩供給。
Country Tax Treatment各國稅務處理
  • Singapore — 0% tax on distributions if REIT distributes 90%+ of taxable income. No capital gains tax. Foreigners same treatment.新加坡 — 若REIT分配90%+應稅所得，分配免稅。無資本利得稅。外國人同等待遇。
  • United States — 30% withholding tax for foreign investors on REIT dividends (reducible via tax treaty). US residents taxed at ordinary income rates.美國 — 外國投資者REIT股息預扣稅30%(可透過稅務協定降低)。美國居民按一般所得稅率課稅。
  • Japan — 15.315% withholding on dividends for non-residents. J-REITs must distribute 90%+ for corporate tax exemption.日本 — 非居民股息預扣稅15.315%。J-REIT須分配90%+以免公司稅。
  • Australia — Distributions taxed as income. Fund payments may include tax-deferred components. Non-residents face managed investment trust withholding of 15%.澳洲 — 分配按所得課稅。基金支付可能包含稅務遞延部分。非居民面臨管理投資信託預扣稅15%。
  • Malaysia — 10% withholding on distributions to individuals. REITs must distribute 90%+ of income. Tax-transparent vehicle.馬來西亞 — 個人分配預扣稅10%。REIT須分配90%+所得。稅務透明工具。
Spread: Yield vs 10yr Bond利差：殖利率 vs 10年期公債
  • What it measures — REIT dividend yield minus the risk-free 10-year government bond yield of the listing country. A positive spread means the REIT pays more than holding government bonds.衡量什麼 — REIT殖利率減去上市國10年期公債殖利率。正利差表示REIT派息高於持有公債。
  • >+2% = attractive risk premium. 0 to +2% = fair compensation. Negative = bond beats REIT on income — you're paying for growth.>+2% = 有吸引力的風險溢酬。0至+2% = 合理補償。負值 = 公債收入勝過REIT——你在為成長付費。
  • Key insight: Japan REITs have the best spreads globally (+2-4%) because JGB 10yr is only 1.05%. Singapore REITs at +2.0-3.0% are also attractive. US REITs often have negative spreads because Treasuries yield 4.25%.關鍵發現：日本REIT利差全球最佳（+2-4%），因為JGB 10年期僅1.05%。新加坡REIT +2.0-3.0%也具吸引力。美國REIT常為負利差，因為美債殖利率4.25%。
REIT vs Direct PropertyREIT vs 直接持有房產
  • Liquidity — REITs trade daily on exchanges. Direct property takes 3-6 months to sell. REITs win on exit flexibility.流動性 — REIT在交易所每日交易。直接持有物業需3-6個月出售。REIT的退出彈性完勝。
  • Diversification — One REIT = portfolio of 50-200 properties. One property = single-asset concentration risk.分散性 — 一檔REIT=50-200個物業的組合。一間房子=單一資產集中風險。
  • Leverage control — Direct property: you choose your LTV. REIT: manager decides gearing (typically 25-45%). Less control but professional management.槓桿控制 — 直接持有：你決定貸款成數。REIT：經理人決定槓桿（通常25-45%）。控制權較少但有專業管理。
  • Volatility — REIT prices swing with stock markets. Direct property: values are sticky but illiquid. Emotional tolerance matters.波動性 — REIT價格隨股市波動。直接持有：房價黏性強但流動性差。情緒承受力很重要。
Red Flags to Watch警訊指標
  • Gearing >45% in a rising rate environment = interest costs eating into DPU. Check interest cover ratio (should be >3x).槓桿率>45%在升息環境中=利息成本侵蝕每單位派息(DPU)。檢查利息保障倍數（應>3倍）。
  • P/NAV >2.0 means you're paying 2x the value of underlying assets. Only justified for the highest-quality growth REITs (Goodman, Equinix).P/NAV >2.0表示你付了底層資產2倍的價格。只有最頂級成長型REIT才合理（Goodman、Equinix）。
  • Payout >100% means the REIT is distributing more than it earns — funded by capital or debt. Unsustainable. Check if it's a one-off or structural.配息率>100%表示REIT派的比賺的多——靠資本或債務支撐。不可持續。確認是一次性還是結構性。
  • Negative spread + high gearing + falling YoY = triple warning. The REIT is losing on income, loaded with debt, and declining in value.負利差+高槓桿+YoY下跌=三重警訊。REIT收入虧損、負債累累、價值下跌。

☐ DPU growth trend — rising or at least stable over 5yr ☐ 利差 >+1.5% vs 10年期公債（收入正當性）
☐ P/NAV <1.0找價值；>1.0僅在成長性合理時
☐ 槓桿率 <40%（升息週期偏好低於35%）

③ Total Return Calculation③ 總報酬計算
REIT Total Return =
Capital gain/loss (buy vs sell price)
+ Cumulative dividends received
− Withholding tax on dividends
− Brokerage & platform fees
± FX gain/loss (cross-border)

Compare this to: simply holding the 10yr bond of the same country for the same period. If the bond wins, the REIT wasn't worth the risk. REIT總報酬 =
資本利得/損失（買入 vs 賣出價）
+ 累計收到的股息
− 股息預扣稅
− 券商與平台手續費
± 匯率損益（跨境投資）

對比：同期間持有同國10年期公債。如果公債贏了，REIT就不值得承擔那個風險。
