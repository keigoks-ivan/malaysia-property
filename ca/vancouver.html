EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Metro Vancouver, with a population of 2.6 million, is Canada's most expensive housing market at an average price of C$1.28 million. The city is geography-constrained — bounded by mountains to the north, the US border to the south, and the Pacific Ocean to the west — creating permanent supply limitations that underpin long-term price support. The market faces a layered regulatory environment: the federal foreign buyer ban (until 2027), British Columbia's speculation and vacancy tax, and the additional property transfer tax on foreign purchases. Vancouver's large Asian diaspora community has historically been a key demand driver, and the foreign ban's impact has been moderated by permanent resident pathways. PTI of 12.5x makes Vancouver the least affordable city in Canada.

大溫哥華地區人口260萬，是加拿大最昂貴的住房市場，平均價格C$128萬。城市受地理限制 — 北面環山、南接美國邊境、西臨太平洋 — 形成永久性供給限制，支撐長期價格。市場面臨多層監管環境：聯邦外國買家禁令（至2027年）、卑詩省投機和空置稅，以及外國人購買的額外物業轉讓稅。溫哥華龐大的亞裔移民社區歷來是關鍵需求驅動力，外國買家禁令的影響因永久居民途徑而得到緩解。房價所得比12.5倍使溫哥華成為加拿大最不可負擔的城市。

PRICE TREND房價走勢

Vancouver prices surged +27% during the 2020–2021 boom before correcting. The market has tracked closely with Toronto in percentage terms but remains approximately C$200K higher in absolute terms due to the geography constraint premium. Notably, Vancouver's correction has been slightly deeper than Toronto's, reflecting the additional impact of provincial foreign buyer taxes layered on top of the federal ban. Prices remain approximately 11% below the 2022 peak.

溫哥華價格在2020至2021年狂潮期間飆升+27%後修正。市場在百分比表現上與多倫多密切跟蹤，但因地理限制溢價在絕對值上高出約C$20萬。值得注意的是，溫哥華的修正略深於多倫多，反映了省級外國買家稅疊加聯邦禁令的額外影響。價格仍比2022年高峰低約11%。

KEY NEIGHBOURHOODS重點社區

West Side is Vancouver's ultra-premium enclave at C$3.5M, historically favoured by affluent Asian buyers, with yields of only 2.0%. Downtown/Yaletown offers the urban condo experience at C$850K with 3.2% yields. Burnaby/Metrotown is the emerging suburban hub at C$780K with 3.5% yields, benefiting from SkyTrain connectivity and the Metrotown redevelopment. Surrey provides the best value at C$950K with 3.8% yields, driven by the Surrey-Langley SkyTrain extension.

西區是溫哥華的超級豪宅區，C$350萬，歷來受富裕亞裔買家青睞，收益率僅2.0%。市中心/耶魯鎮以C$85萬提供都市公寓體驗，收益率3.2%。本拿比/鐵道鎮是新興郊區中心，C$78萬，收益率3.5%，受惠於天車連接和鐵道鎮重建。素里以C$95萬提供最佳價值，收益率3.8%，受素里-蘭里天車延伸推動。

FOREIGN BUYER IMPACT外國買家影響

Vancouver has been Canada's ground zero for foreign buyer policy intervention. The BC government introduced a 20% foreign buyer tax in 2016, followed by the federal ban in 2023, extended to 2027. The layered approach has significantly reduced foreign transaction volumes, particularly from mainland China. However, the impact has been partially offset by buyers obtaining permanent residency before purchasing, and by the continued flow of capital through the investor immigration program. West Side detached homes saw the most significant price impact from these policies, declining 15–20% from 2017–2019 before the pandemic boom erased those losses.

溫哥華一直是加拿大外國買家政策干預的核心。卑詩省政府2016年推出20%外國買家稅，隨後2023年聯邦禁令生效，延長至2027年。多層政策顯著減少了外國交易量，尤其是來自中國大陸的買家。然而，買家在購買前取得永久居民身份，以及投資移民項目的持續資金流入，部分抵消了影響。西區獨棟住宅受這些政策影響最大，2017至2019年下跌15至20%，後因疫情狂潮而抹去跌幅。

TRANSIT & DEVELOPMENT交通與開發

The Surrey-Langley SkyTrain Extension (16 km, 8 stations) is the most significant transit project, extending the Expo Line to Langley City Centre. Construction is underway with completion expected by 2028. The Broadway Subway (UBC extension under planning) would be transformative for the West Side. The Metrotown redevelopment in Burnaby is creating a second downtown with major mixed-use projects around the SkyTrain station, driving the area's price appreciation and densification.

素里-蘭里天車延伸（16公里，8個站點）是最重要的交通項目，將世博線延伸至蘭里市中心。施工進行中，預計2028年完工。百老匯地鐵（UBC延伸規劃中）將對西區產生變革性影響。本拿比的鐵道鎮重建正在天車站周圍打造第二個市中心，大型混合用途項目推動該區域價格升值和高密度化。

RENTAL MARKET租賃市場

Vancouver's rental market is among the most expensive in Canada, with average one-bedroom rents at approximately C$2,600/month. Despite high rents, gross yields of 3.1% are below the BOC rate, reflecting the extreme price levels. The rental market benefits from strong demand driven by immigration, international students, and tech sector employment. BC's rent control framework (maximum annual increases tied to inflation) provides stability for tenants but limits income growth for landlords.

溫哥華租賃市場是加拿大最昂貴之一，一房平均租金約C$2,600/月。儘管租金高企，3.1%的毛租金收益率低於BOC利率，反映極端價格水平。租賃市場受惠於移民、國際學生和科技業就業帶來的強勁需求。卑詩省的租金管控框架（年度最高漲幅與通膨掛鉤）為租戶提供穩定性，但限制了房東的收入增長。

OUTLOOK展望

Base case: Vancouver prices stabilize with +1–3% annual appreciation through 2028, supported by geography constraints and immigration but weighed down by affordability limits and the foreign ban. Bull case: Foreign ban expiry in 2027 plus aggressive BOC cuts release pent-up demand from Asian investors, driving a +10–15% surge in West Side and Downtown. Bear case: Immigration cuts, extended foreign ban, and a tech sector downturn push prices down -5–8% further, with the West Side most exposed due to its historical reliance on foreign capital.

基本情景：溫哥華價格穩定，2028年前年升值+1至3%，受地理限制和移民支撐但受負擔能力和外國買家禁令制約。牛市情景：2027年外國買家禁令到期加上BOC激進降息釋放亞裔投資者的積壓需求，推動西區和市中心+10至15%急漲。熊市情景：移民削減、禁令延長和科技業低迷推動價格再跌-5至8%，西區因歷史上依賴外國資本而風險最大。
