EXECUTIVE SUMMARY執行摘要

Canada's housing market in 2026 is in a delicate transition. After the historic COVID boom (+21% in 2021) and subsequent correction (-7.5% in 2022), prices have stabilized nationally but diverge sharply by city. Toronto (-1.5% YoY) and Vancouver (-2.5%) remain under pressure from high rates and the foreign buyer ban, while Calgary (+5%) and Edmonton (+4%) benefit from affordability migration.

The BOC has cut rates from 5.0% to 3.25% since June 2024, with further cuts expected. The federal foreign buyer ban, extended to 2027, has cooled international demand but immigration targets of 500K/year create structural demand. Canada's chronic housing supply deficit (~3.5M units by 2030 per CMHC) remains the dominant long-term theme.

加拿大2026年房地產市場處於微妙的轉型期。經歷歷史性COVID繁榮（2021年+21%）和隨後的調整（2022年-7.5%）後，全國價格已穩定但城市間差異顯著。多倫多（-1.5%）和溫哥華（-2.5%）受高利率和外國買家禁令壓制，而卡加利（+5%）和艾德蒙頓（+4%）則受益於負擔能力遷移。

加拿大銀行自2024年6月起已從5.0%降息至3.25%，預計將繼續降息。延長至2027年的聯邦外國買家禁令冷卻了國際需求，但每年50萬移民目標創造結構性需求。CMHC估計至2030年加拿大住房供應缺口約350萬套，仍是主導的長期主題。

MACRO & IMMIGRATION宏觀與移民

Canada's economy grows at 1.5% (2025), constrained by high rates but supported by immigration. The government targets 500K permanent residents annually — creating ~200K new household formations/year. Housing starts at ~240K/year fall far short. The CMHC estimates Canada needs 5.8M new homes by 2030 to restore affordability. This supply-demand gap underpins long-term price support despite cyclical weakness.

加拿大經濟增長1.5%（2025年），受高利率制約但移民支持。政府目標每年50萬永久居民——每年創造約20萬新家庭。住房開工量約24萬/年遠不及需求。CMHC估計加拿大至2030年需新建580萬套住房以恢復負擔能力。這一供需缺口為長期價格提供支撐，儘管有週期性弱勢。

FOREIGN BUYER BAN外國買家禁令

The Prohibition on the Purchase of Residential Property by Non-Canadians Act took effect January 2023, extended to January 2027. Exemptions exist for PR holders, refugee claimants, and work permit holders buying under C$500K. The ban has measurably cooled Vancouver and Toronto luxury markets where Chinese buyers were historically active. Some investors use corporate structures or PR pathways to circumvent restrictions.

《禁止非加拿大人購買住宅物業法》2023年1月生效，延長至2027年1月。永久居民、難民申請人和工作簽證持有者購買50萬加元以下物業可豁免。禁令明顯冷卻了中國買家歷來活躍的溫哥華和多倫多豪宅市場。部分投資者通過公司結構或PR途徑規避限制。

TORONTO OVERVIEW多倫多概覽

Canada's largest city and financial capital. Condo market under pressure — investor exits rising, rental vacancies increasing from 1% to 3%. Detached homes more resilient. GTA population 7M+. Transit-oriented development (Ontario Line, GO expansion) reshaping suburban premiums.

加拿大最大城市和金融首都。公寓市場承壓——投資者退出增加，租賃空置率從1%升至3%。獨棟住宅更具韌性。大多倫多地區人口700萬+。以交通為導向的發展（Ontario Line、GO擴展）正在重塑郊區溢價。

VANCOUVER OVERVIEW溫哥華概覽

Canada's most expensive market constrained by geography (mountains + ocean). Foreign buyer ban hit hardest here. Provincial speculation tax and vacancy tax layers on top of federal ban. Detached homes in West Side average C$3.5M+. Pre-sale condo market soft. Strong Asian diaspora community creates latent demand if restrictions ease.

POLICY FRAMEWORK政策框架

Multi-layered: federal foreign buyer ban, BOC stress test (qualifying rate 5.25% or contract+2%), provincial speculation taxes (BC, ON), municipal vacancy taxes, first-time buyer incentives (FHSA, HBP). Current government pivoting from restriction to supply-side — fast-tracking permits, federal housing fund, zoning reform pressure on municipalities.

多層次：聯邦外國買家禁令、加拿大銀行壓力測試（合格利率5.25%或合約+2%）、省級投機稅（BC、ON）、市級空置稅、首購族優惠（FHSA、HBP）。現任政府從限制轉向供給側——加快許可、聯邦住房基金、向市政府施壓進行分區改革。

OUTLOOK 2026–2028展望 2026–2028

BOC expected to cut to 2.75% by end-2026. This should revive sales activity by 10–15%. Toronto/Vancouver prices likely flat to +3% as rate relief offsets supply additions. Calgary/Edmonton may see 3–5% growth on affordability migration. Key catalyst: foreign buyer ban expiry in 2027 could reignite Vancouver luxury. Risk: immigration policy tightening, recession, or rates staying higher for longer.

預計加拿大銀行2026年底前降至2.75%。這應使銷售活動恢復10–15%。多倫多/溫哥華價格可能持平至+3%，因利率下降抵銷供應增加。卡加利/艾德蒙頓因負擔能力遷移可能增長3–5%。關鍵催化劑：2027年外國買家禁令到期可能重燃溫哥華豪宅市場。風險：移民政策收緊、衰退或利率維持較高水準更久。
